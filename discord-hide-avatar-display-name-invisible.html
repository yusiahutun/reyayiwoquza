How to Make Your Display Name and Avatar on Discord Invisible for More Privacy

By using a special Unicode character that Discord accepts but cannot render, it is possible to make your display name appear blank. Likewise, by using a transparent image, you can make your avatar appear blank or completely invisible. If you've noticed some of your friends using blank usernames or avatars, here's how to make them invisible on your profile.

How to Make Your Display Name Invisible on Discord

To conceal your display name in Discord, follow the below steps.

  • Go to the Unicode Explorer website and click on the Copy button to copy the special character with the codepoint U+1CBC.
  • Open Discord and click the cog wheel icon in the lower left corner.
  • Navigate to the My Account tab on the left.
  • Click Edit next to your Display Name.
  • Delete the existing display name and paste the special character previously copied in the Display Name field.
  • Click Save Changes to change your display name to a blank field.

    • Discord used to accept special characters in the username field, and users were able to make their username blank. Discord does not accept special characters in the username field anymore, so it is impossible to use Unicode characters to make the username invisible.

    How to Make Your Avatar Blank or Invisible in Discord

    To make your avatar look invisible, you need to change your Discord profile picture and set a transparent image.

    You can create a transparent image in Paint 3D, GIMP, or even Canva.

    Once you have a transparent image saved, follow these steps:

  • Open Discord and click the cog wheel icon in the lower left corner.
  • Navigate to the My Account tab on the left.
  • Click on the Edit User Profile button.
  • Click the Change Avatar button and then click Upload Image.
  • Select the transparent picture you created and click Open to upload it.
  • Skip the Edit Image window.
  • Click Save Changes.

    If you make your display name and avatar blank, your friends may be unable to identify you.

    Surprise Your Friends With a Blank Username and Display Name

    With the help of a transparent image and a special Unicode character, you can make your avatar and display name blank on Discord.
