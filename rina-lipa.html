Table of Biography

Rina Lipa is a British Actress, Dancer, Model, Businesswoman, and Instagram Personality. Rina Lipa is popular for being the younger sister of Dua Lipa who is a well-known singer, songwriter, and model. Dua Lipa is also a three-time Grammy winner for “BEST POP VOCAL ALBUM”.

Early Life and Childhood

Rina Lipa was born on May 14, 2001, in London, England, United Kingdom. She is 22 years old and is under the astrological sign, Taurus. She holds British nationality and is of Albanian descent.

Her father’s name is Dukagjin Lipa and her mother’s name is Anesa (née Rexha) Lipa. She also has two siblings and their names are Dua Lipa and Gjin Lipa. Her mother is a tourism manager whereas her father is a marketing manager.

He is the founder and CEO of Republika Communications Agency. Moreover, her parents are Albanian and fled Pristina in the year 1992. And, Historian Seit Lipa and the former head of the ‘Kosovo Institute of History’ is her paternal grandfather.

Education

Regarding Lipa’s educational background and qualifications, she has been attending Sylvia Young part-time, alongside doing A-levels in media, psychology, and drama.

She attended the ballet institute and was able to perform for several in-house productions. Likewise, Rina spent 6 years at the ‘Sylvia Young Theatre School’. She eventually was able to master the Stanislavski technique.

Career and Professional Life

Rina Lipa was featured in a commercial for the telecommunication company ‘Ipko’ in the year 2006. Then, two years later, she was able to become part of the ‘Business Loans Campaign’ for “Raiffeisen Bank”.

In the year 2009, the ‘Flour Fortification Campaign’ was her first collaboration with UNICEF. Also, she has been able to work for the organization’s anti-smoking campaign, ‘Heart Attack’ in the year 2013. Likewise, in the year 2010, she starred in a commercial for her father’s company, “Republika Communications Agency”.

She went on to lend her voice to an Albanian voice-over project for ‘Rugove’ a company based in Kosovo in the year 2014. She also was able to receive the ‘Jack Petchey Award’ in the same year, which recognizes exceptional young individuals in London and Essex.

On top of that, she was able to showcase her dancing skills by participating in The Rock Challenge competition in the year 2015.

Moreover, moving on to 2019, she got the opportunity to collaborate with ‘Nasty Gal’. It is a fast-fashion brand under ‘Boohoo.com’ to create a capsule collection inspired by her personal style and the iconic 1990s.

Rina Lipa’s talents extend beyond music and fashion, as she has also dabbled in acting with a short film and has upcoming auditions lined up, indicating a busy schedule ahead.

She occasionally tags companies and entities in her social media posts. And, during ‘London Fashion Week’ in September 2017, she supported emerging designer Adele Baron by showcasing her designs through a photo of herself wearing them.
