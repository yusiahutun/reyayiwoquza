Giacomo Baessato Movies And TV Shows

When Giacomo Baessato was eight years old, his parents enrolled him in acting classes at a nearby Film/TV school, and that is when he began performing as an actor. In a 1999 episode of the American-Canadian television series So Weird, he made his acting debut.

His first film role was a small one in the 2000 horror comedy Scary Movie. He then provided the voice for the action-adventure character Enzo Matrix in the Canadian CGI-animated television series ReBoot. He was cast as a thirteen-year-old in the science fiction horror picture Dreamcatcher, which was written and directed by Lawrence Kasdan, who also co-penned the screenplays for Raiders of the Lost Ark and three Star Wars films.

Giacomo continued his education at the Circle in the Square Theater School in New York City after finishing high school. He spent three years working primarily on stage while residing in New York.

Over the years, he has made appearances in a number of well-known TV series, such as Motive, The X Files, Continuum, The 100, Supernatural, Arrow, The Whispers, Wayward Pines, Travelers, and Fringe. Additionally, he performed the recurring parts of Officer Kearn in the crime drama television series Gracepoint, Russ Roche in the fourth season of the television series iZombie, and Shane Slater in the supernatural drama television series The Returned.
