Who Is Michael Kurowski? His Age, Job, Wife

Meet Michael Kurowski, father of Magnolia Network’s Inn the Works host Lindsey Kurowski. After a successful first season which premiered in early 2021, the show has returned for season 2 in 2022. If you’re a first-timer, then you should know that Lindsey restores historic motels and inns across the country.

In the first season, Lindsey also purchased the Oak Knoll Lodge in Big Bear Lake, Calif., determined to renovate it and give it the life it needed. Now, in season 2, Lindsey makes her way to the East Coast where she renovates Springs Inn, a 40-room historic motel in her hometown of Berkshires in Massachusetts where her family still lives.

In an interview, Lindsey had confirmed that her family was working together to fix up the retro 40-room motel. She  explained to House Beautiful, “I absolutely called in some favors to my longest friends and my dad. You may also catch a glimpse of my brother rollerblading in an empty pool and my grandmother getting forklift-certified — the usual.”

“You’ll see a person slightly out of their comfort zone as the new motel is twice the size of the Oak Knoll Lodge,” she told the outlet.

Since we can expect the whole family to be involved in this season’s project let’s learn a few things ahead about them. Below you’ll learn about her father Michael where we cover his marriage, age, job, and current residence.

Meet Lindsey Kurowski Dad, Michael Kurowski

Michael Kurowski and his ex-wife Stacey Gilfillan were parents to four children. Stacey is an employee at Eli Lilly and Company. She works there as an executive pharmaceutical sales representative since March 2018 in San Antonio, Texas. She has been associated with the company for over 15 years.

Stacey is currently 56 years old while as of March 2022 at the time of this article.

Lindsey is the face of the Magnolia show. She is the owner of Knotty Pine Print & Design since January 2014.

Lindsey had previously worked as director of marketing at Aosa and before that worked in sales & marketing for Nature Woven. She is a graduate of Westfield State University.

Besides Lindsey, he also has a daughter named Kelsey Kurowski. Kelsey turned 32 years old in May 2021. She works as an accountant at Challenger Cables Sales. His son Matt Kurowski who is a graduate of Southwick High School and also lends hand to his sister’s enterprise every once in a while.

Adam the youngest of the four Adam turned 22-year-old in December 2021. Adam appears with his sister on Inn the Works. He can be seen helping out his sister on the show a few clips of which has been shared on Instagram.

Are Michael Kurowski And Stacey Kurowski Still Married?

Michael Kurowski and Stacey Kurowski are no longer married. Their marriage ended in divorce sometime before 2008. Now his ex-wife, Stacey married back on 17 May 2008 to her husband Christopher “Gilly” Gilfillan. Born on 19 October 1955, Christopher is 66 years old.

According to his Facebook, Chris comes from a family of military. Like his father, LTC John P. WW2 was a pacific veteran who he remembers on his Facebook on veterans day. More about his family, he is a grandfather of 6 grandchildren.

Who Is Michael Kurowski Wife?

Michael Kurowski also married but almost 9 years later after his wife. He tied the knots in 2017 but hasn’t shared details regarding his wife. But, based on our research, Michael could be married to Kimberley Wendling. A resident of Hinsdale, Massachusetts, Kimberley is currently 56 years old.

Michael Kurowski Age

Born on 16 April 1958, Michael Kurowski reached the age of 63 in 2021.

Michael Kurowski Job

In June 2017, Lindsey gave a little insight into her father Michael Kurowski’s job. For the past 35 years at the time of the post, Michael worked as a Lineman.

Lindsey captioned the post, “Through sub-zero nor’easter storms & volunteering weeks in Louisiana after Katrina to give people power/water. To coming home with no eyebrows because a transformer exploded & that time on Christmas when he tripped while operating a chainsaw to get a fallen tree off a power line. He is the definition of hard work & I keep my collar mostly blue because of him. The union head of Local 455, we salute you”.

He retired from the job in June 2017. According to Salary.com, Lineman makes a salary of nearly $90,048 a year.
