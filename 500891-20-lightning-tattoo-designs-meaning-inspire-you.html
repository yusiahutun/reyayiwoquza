20 best lightning tattoo designs with meaning to inspire you

Many people acknowledge nature and use symbolism to express themselves through different lightning tattoo designs inked on their skin. These tattoos are a representation of the primal force of nature. They are mostly associated with emotions, strength, clarity, inspiration, and creativity. Regardless of your choice, lighting bolt tattoos will make you stand out from the crowd and appear super cool.

What does a lightning tattoo mean? It can mean several things to several people and cultures. However, it is mostly associated with the Greek God Zeus, a symbol of strength, enlightenment, intelligence, and intuition. In some cultures, this tattoo is seen as a symbol of thrill and excitement and a way to ward off evil spirits.

In other cultures, it attracts positive energy or good luck. However, one of the most important meanings behind lightning bolt is fertility. Lightning often preceded rain showers, making crops grow and keeping the earth fertile and healthy.
