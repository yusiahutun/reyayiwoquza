Beyonc and Jay Z Share Photos From Family Vacation

Even Bey and Jay need a break sometimes.

After their show in Poland, where Beyoncé found herself stuck on stage, the Carters took a well-deserved mini-vacation with their oldest daughter, Blue Ivy.

Bey took to Instagram to flaunt her off-duty style in a flowy orange floral dress and Ritch Erani PVC heels. She posted a photo and a video of her and husband Jay-Z relaxing and smiling on stone steps, with her shoes and a beer thrown to the side.

She then showed off her view of the water from a boat with smile-y family photo of her, Jay, and Blue. The only thing missing from the family shot are one-year-old twins Rumi and Sir.

In another flawless pic, Bey is seen reaching for Blue's hand on a dock overlooking the water.

Beyoncé didn't mention where they're spending their time off, but their next scheduled show is for July 3 in Cologne, Germany.

The Carters time off is very well-deserved, as they have been working nonstop. Their "On The Run II" tour began at the beginning of June, with tours all over the world until October. They also managed to drop a surprise album, "Everything Is Love," in mid-June.

Getty Images

Blue Ivy has been traveling with her parents while they tour, and has been spotted in the audience of multiple shows. She was most memorably caught closing her eyes while NSFW videos of her parents played on the screens.

Blue also sings, dances, and interacts with the crowd during the shows, taking after her famous parents.

Beyoncé and Jay-Z are often very strict about what they share about their personal lives and children, so these photos are a welcome peek into their time off. Recently, they have been more open to giving an intimate look into their lives—their tour videos even include footage of their vow renewal and never-before-seen sexy photos in bed.
