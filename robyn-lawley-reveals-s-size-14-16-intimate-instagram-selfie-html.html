Robyn Lawley reveals she's a size 14-16 in intimate Instagram selfie

She’s the Australian model known for her fabulous physique and her push for body diversity in the fashion industry.

And on Wednesday Robyn Lawley uploaded a saucy selfie to Instagram, where she revealed both her sensational figure and her dress size.

The statuesque 27-year-old revealed she is a size US 12 - or an Australian size 14-16.

Revealing: Robyn Lawley looked sensational in her intimate Instagram post, as she showed off her ample cleavage wearing nothing but a sports bra and frilly underwear

In the black and white picture, Robyn displayed in her racy side, wearing nothing but a black sports bra and lace knickers.

The brunette cropped her mirror selfie below her eyes, so that her body was the primary focus of the photo.

The intimate image showed off the model’s ample cleavage and long locks, as she pouted for the camera with her mouth slightly agape.

'Feeling strong': Robyn is a champion for size diversity in the fashion industry, and captioned her latest snap with a bicep emoji

She captioned the photo ‘Feeling strong...Size US 12 – Australian 14-16. 6ft2 and proud, accompanied by the bicep emoji.

Robyn also used the hashtag #sizediversity.

After she posted the photo, the model was inundated with comments from admiring fans, calling her ‘beautiful’ and 'an inspiration’.

The Sydney-born stunner has long championed a plethora of body sizes in the fashion industry. 

Flawless physique: Sydney-born Robyn has rocked runways across the world

Just recently Robyn urged European fashion bosses to eliminate the term 'plus-size' from the modelling world.

In an interview with The Daily Telegraph Robyn said, 'labeling me plus-size is going to unfortunately segregate me from the other models. I'm marked as apart.'

On the same day she posted her selfie to Instagram, Robyn was in Cancun, Mexico for a photo shoot.

The brunette sizzled on the sand as she rode a horse along the shores wearing nothing but a blue bikini.
