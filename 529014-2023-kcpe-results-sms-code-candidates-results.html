2023 KCPE Results: SMS Code Through Which Candidates Can Get Results

  • The Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC) announced the release of an SMS code for accessing Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) results
  • Candidates can retrieve their results by sending an SMS containing their index number to 40054; the SMS costs KSh 25
  • Education Cabinet Secretary Ezekiel Machogu is poised to unveil the results for the 1,415,315 candidates who took the KCPE exams earlier this month

Nairobi - The Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC) has released the SMS Code through which the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) candidates can get their results.

This comes as Education Cabinet Secretary Ezekiel Machogu is set to release the results to the 1,415,315 candidates who sat for the KCPE exams at the beginning of this month.

To access a candidate's results, send an SMS with his or her index number to 40054. Every SMS will cost KSh 25.
