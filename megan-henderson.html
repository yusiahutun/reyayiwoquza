Megan Henderson (born February 19, 1975) is an American broadcast journalist. Henderson joined KTLA, the CW affiliate in Los Angeles, in March 2009 and anchors the station's weekday morning news. Prior to joining KTLA, she anchored the morning news at Fox station KDFW in Dallas and at Fox station KSTU in Salt Lake City. She also guest-hosted several episodes of the Fox News Channels Fox & Friends Weekend in 2008.
