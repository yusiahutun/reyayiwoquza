Career and Professional Life

Moving on to his career, he started his career in theater performing in various productions in New York City. Then he went on to make his television debut in the soap opera titled The Guiding Light in 2000. Since then, he has appeared in several television shows and films showcasing his versatility as an actor.

Similarly, he had recurring roles on television as Dr. Matthew Shaw in the Fox series Justice from 2006 to 2007, as Stan Edwards in the CBS drama Hawaii Five-O from 2011 to 2012, as Agent Cameron Davies in the TNT series Rizzoli & Isles in 2016, as Roy in the Netflix Comedy Grace and Frankie from 2018 to 2019, and so on.

Likewise, he has also starred in films such as Riverworld, The Wish List, Tarzan, Tides of War, Never Say, Macbeth, The Wedding Chapel, Switched for Christmas, etc. Also, he has voiceover in several video games such as Call of Duty 3, God of War: Ghost of Sparta, Metal Gear Solid: Peace Walker, and Halo Wars 2.

Furthermore, he played the role of Trammell Thatcher in the Fox nighttime drama titled Lone Star, and as Nicholas Deering in the Lifetime comedy-drama Devious Maids. In addition to his work as an actor, he has also worked as a fight coordinator and stunt performer, helping to choreograph fight scenes in various productions.
