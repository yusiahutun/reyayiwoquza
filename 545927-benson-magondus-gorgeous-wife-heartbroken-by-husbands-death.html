Benson Magondus Gorgeous Wife Heartbroken by Husbands Death

  • Major George Benson Magondu's family was left grieving the loss of their loved one after the West Pokot chopper crash
  • Magondu's family spoke after their kin's demise, revealing that selfless and humble man whose generosity extended to the entire neighbourhood
  • The widow of the late Magondu took to social media to mourn her loving husband in touching clips, saying she did not know how to live without her soulmate

The late Major George Benson Magondu's widow has mourned him in a touching post.

The tragic crash of the KDF helicopter, which claimed the lives of Chief of Defence Forces General Francis Ogolla and nine other KDF soldiers, left the family of Major George Benson Magondu devastated.

Magondu, a 29-year-old pilot, was one of the casualties in the Chesugon crash in Elgeyo-Marakwet on April 18.

In videos his wife shared online, the young woman who owns a baby shop mourned his demise.

She shared videos of the family while reminiscing on their good times.

In one of the videos, the late soldier spent time with his kids in their living room. In another video, Magondu and his wife drove around in their car.

How Magondu's family learnt of his demise

His mother, Mary Wambui, recounted how they learned of the crash through Magondu's wife, who was also a military captain.

They were informed that he had sustained a severe facial injury and was rushed to the hospital, where he was later pronounced dead.

According to Citizen TV, Wambui stated that her son has left a wide gap.

"The gap is big because George was a family man. At 29, he acted like a 40-year-old father. He loved the family, the kids, and his spouse. He could sacrifice all his time and money to keep the children well, said Wambui.
