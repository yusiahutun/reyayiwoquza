Sweet Home Malibu! Take a Tour of Reese Witherspoons Former California Farmhouse

Reese Witherspoon has lived in countless gorgeous homes throughout her life. One of her most magnificent properties to date is her former Malibu farmhouse, where the Big Little Lies star resided with her husband, Jim Toth, and her kids.

Reese and the talent agent purchased the rustic property, called Zuma Farms, in June 2019, Variety reported. The Little Fires Everywhere actress paid $6.25 million for the California residence, which sat on two acres and boasted endless amounts of space.

More than a year after settling into the home, however, Reese and Jim sold the estate in November 2020. According to the Dirt, the couple unloaded the house in an off-market deal for just over $6.7 million, making a profit of nearly $500,000.

As the outlet reported, a woman named Meg Haney, the divorced heiress and daughter of Chattanooga real estate billionaire Franklin Haney, purchased the extraordinary pad. Fitted with three bedrooms, three bathrooms, a spacious kitchen, living rooms and more, there’s no doubt it wasn’t easy for the Legally Blonde alum to say goodbye.

The Academy Award winner’s Malibu property included more than just their main house. The plot also boasted a guest home, a horse stable and multiple outbuildings. At the time they were still living there, Reese often shared pics of her famous family lounging in their backyard or hanging with their animals.

On top of all that, Reese and Jim’s abode was fitted with other amenities. It had an art and yoga studio, a private screening room with a bar area and a patio with outdoor entertainment. In the backyard, they were surrounded by dozens of ancient sycamore trees and a vegetable garden.

The Morning Show actress, who shares her older kids, Ava and Deacon, with ex-husband Ryan Phillippe, and son Tennessee with Jim, is always traveling the world for her Hollywood career, so nothing makes Reese happier than when she’s home with her children.

The Golden Globe recipient is especially grateful for R&R with her family at times when she’s struggling to juggle her career and life as a mom. Fortunately, Reese — who owns homes in Los Angeles, Nashville and the Bahamas, per Variety — gets some support from her children.

“I’m just trying to hold on, trying to make it through. My older kids help with the little one,” she told InStyle in November 2016. “It’s nice to have a big family. My mom worked, and I think it’s good for kids to see women working and being successful.”
