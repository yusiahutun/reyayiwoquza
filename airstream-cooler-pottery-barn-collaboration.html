Airstream and Pottery Barn Collaborated on a New Line of Home Decor That Includes a Camper-Shaped Co

What do you get when an iconic brand like Airstream partners with an equally iconic home decor brand like Pottery Barn? A collection of summer must-haves that's fit for wanderlust spirits and homebodies alike.

We're big fans of summer cocktails, backyard singalongs, and—when safe to do so—nice, long road trips. So naturally, when the adventure enthusiasts at Airstream teamed up with the always-swoon-worthy Pottery Barn brand for a special collab just in time for warm weather, we hopped right on the bandwagon (err, trailer?).

Airstream Party Cooler

Airstream Party Cooler

The Airstream X Pottery Barn collection launches May 15 and features everything from framed art prints and lounge-worthy throw pillows to—our favorite—a camper-shaped drink cooler that will fit righttt in on that dreamy backyard patio where we've been spending so much of our time recently.

With a 4-quart capacity (it'll hold about 8-10 bottles or 10-12 cans) and a hardy aluminum finish inspired by the original Airstream designs from the 1930s and 40s, the cooler is made for celebrating wherever you call home—whether it's on the road or, as is more likely these days, in your backyard. (And it seems camper coolers are having a bit of a moment right now, so all the more reason to join the movement!)

What's more, the new line pays homage to the classic Airstream shape with salt and pepper shakers, a bottle opener, and even a doormat, so you can truly give any space that adventure-chic vibe. Wheels up, y'all!
