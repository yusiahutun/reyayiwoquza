Hugh Jackman Announces The Man. The Music. The Show. World Tour

(CelebrityAccess) – Academy Award®-nominated, Golden Globe and Tony Award-winning performer, Hugh Jackman, has today (Nov 29) announced his first world tour.

‘The Man. The Music. The Show.’ will see Jackman performing hit songs from The Greatest Showman, Les Miserable and more from Broadway and film, accompanied by a live orchestra.

Following a run in the UK and Europe, the 22-city North American leg will begin Tuesday, June 18th at Toyota Center in Houston, TX, with stops in Chicago, Toronto, Boston, Las Vegas, and New York, with two shows at Madison Square Garden, as well as two performances at the Hollywood Bowl in Los Angeles.

Tickets for the North American shows go on sale Friday, December 7th at 10 AM local time at HughJackmanTheShow.com.
