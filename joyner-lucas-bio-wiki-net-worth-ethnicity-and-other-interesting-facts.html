Joyner Lucas Bio, Wiki, Net Worth, Ethnicity and Other Interesting Facts

The expression of talent has different forms. Music, one of the most popular forms of expression, has many fine intellectuals who have given their lives to be servants of this form, like Gary Maurice Lucas Jr. with the pseudonym Joyner Lucas. This songwriter, poet, singer, and rapper is currently under contract with Atlantic Records and has made a name for himself since his professional entrance into music in the early 2000s.

Would you like to learn more about Joyner Lucas, his background, his financial situation, and other interesting facts? Come with us…

Joyner Lucas Bio and Ethnicity

Joyner began his career as a G-Storm when he started rapping at the age of ten. Inspired by the works of Method Man, Nas, The Notorious B.I.G., and Eminem, he later made his first poster music with the latter.

He started rapping as a craft and later formed a group called Film Skool Rejects. At this time Joyner was working with his uncle and they released a mixtape called Workprint: The Greatest Mixtape of All Time within the first year of the group’s formation. The group released their mixtape in 2007, then Joyner went on stage with the stage name Future Joyner and released his first mixtape under the stage name in 2011. However, he had to drop it when the rapper Future became famous. To create a unique identity for himself, Joyner thought it wise to delete “Future” from his stage name and simply call himself Joyner Lucas.

Joyner is currently under contract with Atlantic Records and has experienced a significant rise in his profile. It is the blossoming of a journey that began when he attended South High Community School in Worcester. As he sat in his room listening to Eminem’s voice from his player, the songwriter might not have expected to be on track with his idol one day. He could be heard on the white rapper’s album Kamikaze, released in 2018. The song they did together (Lucky You) debuted at number 6 on the Billboard Hot 100.

The American rapper, who was born on August 17, 1988, in Worcester, Massachusetts, has released four solo mixtapes to date, with his third and fourth mixtape receiving critical acclaim. His mixtape Along with Came Joyner, released in 2015, featured one of his more popular songs, Ross Capicchioni. It was such a success that BET considered him worthy of inclusion in the 2015 BET Hip Hop Awards Cypher. Another mixtape he released in 2017 made it to #7 on the Heatseekers album charts on Billboard.

Since his breakthrough into the mainstream, Joyner Lucas has worked with a few established artists. He has a mixtape called Angels and Demons with Chris Brown, which will be released soon. On February 26, 2018, he released a single from the project. Another important collaboration was with Eminem on his Kamikaze album. The song “Lucky You” debuted at number 6 on the Billboard Hot 100 and was awarded platinum in October 2018. He has collaborated with other artists such as Tech N9ne, Boi-1da, Logic, and Busta Rhymes. Joyner Lucas is currently working on an album called ADHD. He is an African American.
