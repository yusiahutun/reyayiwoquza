Rihanna Wears Pink Sheer Lingerie in Savage x Fenty's Spring Campaign

  • Rihanna stars in the latest Savage x Fenty campaign and BAZAAR.com has the exclusive pictures, featuring the "Love on the Brain" in a sheer pink corset in one photo and a white lacy set in another.
  • On March 1, Savage x Fenty will drop new pieces from its spring collection, with prices ranging from $12.50 to $49.
  • The brand's Xtra VIP box, called T&A All Day, also includes more tantalizing options for buyers.

Spring is a time of revitalization and rebirth, and what better way to resurrect us from our winter hibernation than new photos of Rihanna in lingerie?

The Grammy winning-star looked poised and unbothered in the latest Savage x Fenty campaign, and BAZAAR.com has the exclusive flicks. In one photo, the fashion mogul dons a sheer pink corset while lounging on a lavender and gold chaise–the epitome of elegance. In another, she wears a matching white lace set while shrouding her body in tulle.

All of the garments Rih models are included in Savage x Fenty's spring collection, which officially drops on March 1. Other new items include lingerie staples (i.e. bras, bralettes, undies, and nighties) and some toys that can really help revive your bedroom life (or, as Rihanna sings, "Sticks and stones may break my bones, but chains and whips excite me"). The spring collection is flooded with colors of the Easter egg variety, from pastel yellows, lavenders, and pinks to neon orange and green.

Dennis Leupold

The collection includes sizes from 32A to 42DD and XS to 3X, while prices range from $12.50 to $49. Savage x Fenty is also dropping a new Xtra VIP Box, called T&A All Day. The box, which costs $50, will include a vintage lace cupless bralette and a vintage lace open bikini and thong.
