Vanessa Murdock Biography

Vanessa Murdock is an American TV broadcaster and a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist. Further, she is also a member of the American Meteorological Society and also of the National Weather Association.

Career as an Anchor & Metrologist

She then worked for 2 years as an evening weather anchor for ABC and FOX affiliates in May 2005, based in Charlottesville. Then in November of 2006, she moved from Charlottesville to join the Viper Weather team.

Vanessa worked as a weekend metrologist for News Channel 3 (CBS) for five years in Norfolk in June 2007. Apart from working as a broadcast metrologist, she also taught Metrology at Virginia Wesleyan College since 2007.

Vanessa currently works as a broadcaster meteorologist at the CBS2 weather Team alongside Chris Wragge, Dana Tyler, and Steve Johnson's wife Kristine Johnson. Murdock has been nominated for the Emmy Awards for her contribution to the field of Meteorology in television.
