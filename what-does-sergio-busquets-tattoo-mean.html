Biography of Sergio Busquets

Sergio Busquets Burgos is a Spanish professional footballer who currently plays as a defensive midfielder for Barcelona and the Spanish national team. He was born on July 16, 1988, in Sabadell, Spain.

Busquets began his footballing journey at the young age of six with a local team named CD Badia del Valles. Later, he joined the youth ranks of Barcelona and progressed through the La Masia academy system. Busquets’ talent and skills were quickly recognized, and he was promoted to Barcelona B team in 2007. A year later, he made his first-team debut in a game against Racing Santander.

Since his debut, Busquets has established himself as one of the best defensive midfielders in the world, known for his ability to read the game, intercept passes, and control the tempo of the match. He has won numerous accolades throughout his career, including eight La Liga titles, seven Copa del Rey titles, and three UEFA Champions League titles.
