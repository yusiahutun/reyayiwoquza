Taylor Swift is the perfect BFF to get celebrities past messy breakups

Taylor Swift and her celebrity pals are truly friendship goals, especially when it comes to mending broken hearts. The Miss Americana and Heartbreak Prince crooner has gone through quite a few heartbreaks of her own, and she seems the best fit for ‘healing’ traumas!

From Selena Gomez to Sophie Turner, Taylor Swift’s friend circle is star-studded. Her celebrity friends can vouch for the fact that the 12-time-Grammy-award winner not only knows how to throw a party with Vodka Cranberry, but is also the friend in need when going through a tough time. We’re calling you, Taylor Swift, next time!

Taylor Swift is the perfect BFF to mend celebrities’ broken hearts

The ‘Happiness’ hitmaker heals broken wounds with not just her songs, but also with her presence. The pop music wizard, who has written some of the most popular heartbreak anthems of our times, including Clean, Long Live, The 1, Evermore, Death by a Thousand Cuts, and more, also stood tall next to her friends who needed her during a tough time or two.

Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift

For Swifties, there can be no perfect example of friendship better than Selena and Taylor. The start of their friendship was as epic as their growing bond over the years. Both pop icons dated the Jonas Brothers Joe and Nick at some point in the 2010s and bonded pretty quickly over lyrics and life.

Selena called Taylor her ‘only friend’ at one point and explained how Tay had been there.  

“She was frustrated when I was frustrated; she was sad when I was sad,” she said. ”I think [my friends] visibly saw me in so much pain. And they didn’t want it for me but they—sorry—they never stopped loving me.”

Taylor has never also never shied away from expressing her love for the Single Soon singer. Selena also praised Tay with: “The things that she’s overcome, the situations she’s risen above, it’s been a long time since we were friends and she’s the best. She’s just the best.”

Taylor Swift and Sophie Turner

Taylor and Sophie Turner have been friends for a while and, despite sharing an ex, have grown quite close. While Sophie is currently going through a topsy-turvy divorce with Joe Jonas, Taylor is there for her, with no gesture being too small. Recently, the two have been spotted together in New York City along with friend Blake Lively.

Sources also reportedly claim Sophie is staying at a New York City home owned by her friend Taylor Swift.

“Taylor has opened her home to Sophie,” a source told People, adding, “Sophie is welcome there any time. Taylor continues to be a great friend.”

Taylor Swift and Hugh Jackman

Taylor Swift’s friend circle includes Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds, and apparently Hugh Jackman. The Wolverine star is currently going through a divorce with ex-wife Deborra-Lee Furness. Fans were delighted to see the star taking time to uplift his spirits and join Taylor and others at the Kansas City Chiefs versus New York Jets match at MetLife Stadium on October 1.

The movie star also shared a selfie of their time of fun on Instagram posing with Taylor, Blake Lively, and Ryan Reynolds.
