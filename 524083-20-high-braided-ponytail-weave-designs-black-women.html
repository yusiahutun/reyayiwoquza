How long does a high braided ponytail last?

Braided ponytails last about a week or two and can easily be re-done. To make it last longer, tie down your slicked-back hair to keep it flat and wrap the braid into a bun when you sleep. You can also cover it with a bonnet or silk scarf.

How do you style a cute high ponytail?

You can achieve a cute high ponytail by first lifting a small section of your hair near your crown and gently combing backwards to give your hair more volume. Next, gather all of your hair at the back of your crown.

Smooth out your hair with a comb before securing the ponytail with an elastic band. Finally, set the style with hairspray to keep it in place.
