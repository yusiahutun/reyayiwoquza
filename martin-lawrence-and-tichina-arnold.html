About

Tichina Arnold is a 54 year old American Actress. Born Tichina Rolanda Arnold on 28th June, 1969 in Queens, New York City, New York, USA, she is famous for her roles as Pamela "Pam" James in the Fox sitcom Martin, Rochelle Rock in the UPN/CW sitcom Everybody Hates Chris, Judi Mann in the TV Land original sitcom Happily Divorced, Cassie Calloway in the Starz comedy-drama series Survivor’s Remorse, Tina Butler in the CBS sitcom The Neighborhood, and Paulette in the Mzansi Magic prison drama series Lockdown in a career that spans 1982–present and 1983–present. Her zodiac sign is Cancer.

Martin Lawrence is a 58 year old American Actor. Born Martin Fitzgerald Lawrence on 16th April, 1965 in Frankfurt, Hesse, West Germany and educated at Eleanor Roosevelt High School and Friendly High School, he is famous for Martin Payne on Martin, Marcus Burnett in the Bad Boys franchise, Malcolm Turner / Big Momma in the Big Momma's House franchise. His zodiac sign is Aries.
