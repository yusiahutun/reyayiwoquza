Biography

Lulu was born in Lennoxtown, Scotland on November 3, 1948. Scottish singer best known to American music fans for “The Man with the Golden Gun,” the song from the James Bond film of the same name. In 1969, she won the Eurovision Song Contest representing the U.K. with the song “Boom Bang-a-Bang.” Her 1967 hit “The Boat That I Row” was written by Neil Diamond. She got her to start singing with the band Bellrocks as a teenager.
