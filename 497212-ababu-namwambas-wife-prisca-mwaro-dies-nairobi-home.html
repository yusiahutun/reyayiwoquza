Ababu Namwamba's Ex-Wife Prisca Mwaro Dies at Nairobi Home

  • The former wife of Sports Cabinet Secretary Ababu Namwamba Prisca Mwaro has been found dead
  • A close relative confirmed Mwaro's death, and her remains have since been moved to the MP Shah Hospital morgue
  • The deceased celebrated her birthday yesterday, and this morning, her Facebook timeline quickly changed from birthday wishes to rest-in-peace messages

Sports Cabinet Secretary Ababu Namwamba's ex-wife Prisca Mwaro has died just a day after her birthday.

Mwaro's cause of death was not immediately revealed, but a close relative confirmed she had passed on.

Since news of her death emerged this morning, many Kenyans took to social media to mourn her, expressing the pain that she had passed on so soon.

Reports indicated she was found dead in her house this morning, and her remains were taken to the MP Shah Hospital morgue.

The deceased celebrated her birthday yesterday, and this morning, her Facebook timeline quickly changed from birthday wishes to rest in peace messages.

Taking to his social media, the ODM communication director mourned Mwaro, saying the news of her death had taken him aback.

"RIP Madam Priscah N. Mwaro. Sad," he said.

Prisca Mwaro's last social media post

Priscah and Ababu had three children, a daughter and two sons.

The couple's kids live with Ababu, who always shares photos and videos of them on social media.

On January 10, Priscah shared a video of her lastborn son playing play station on Instagram, which was her last post.

In 2021, the mother of three shared photos of her children on different dates, pampering them with love, and in love, she congratulated her firstborn daughter after she was voted a leader of her class.

Ababu Namwamba and Priscah Mwaro's happy times

Earlier TUKO.co.ke featured photos of Ababu and Priscah during their happy times.
