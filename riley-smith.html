Riley Smith

Riley Smith is an American actor known for his roles in the American science fiction TV series, Frequency and also in The Messengers as Mark Plowman. Besides acting, he also began competing in horse shows at the age of 9 and also won a national championship at the age of 16.

The 5 feet 11 inches tall actor Smith has earned a huge fan following who are keen to know about his relationship. So, is Smith already married or dating his girlfriend? To know the details read the article below.

How Was the Childhood of An American Actor, Riley Smith?

Riley Smith was born on April 12, 1978, in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, USA under the zodiac sign Aries. He is the son of Russ Smith (father) and Roxanne Smith (mother). Smith became a skilled horse rider, competing for the first time in a horse show when he was nine years old.

When Riley was 15, he placed in 4th position in the 3-year-old Mares division at the American Quarter Horse Youth World Championship. At the age of 16, he won the American Quarter Horse World Championship. Smith also became chairman of the 50,000-member youth organization when he was 17.

Likewise, Smith went to Alburnett Junior-Senior High School in the year 1997. Though the actor had an interest in horse racing, he intended to earn a college degree to cover horse racing.
