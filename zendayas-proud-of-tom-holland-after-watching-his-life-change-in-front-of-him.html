Zendaya's proud of Tom Holland after 'watching his life change in front of him'

Tomdaya keep their relationship more low-key than many showbiz romances, but when they do gush about each other, they gush hard. Zendaya has said she’s ‘proud’ of her beau Tom Holland for what he’s achieved in his career. Now, the Euphoria star is throwing it back to when they were both “very, very, young,” and we can’t cope.

The celebrity couple met while filming Spider-Man: Homecoming in 2016 and naturally, dating rumors quickly followed. However, it wasn’t until September 2021 when Tom Holland and Zendaya went Instagram official, and they’ve been goals ever since.

Zendaya ‘couldn’t be more proud’ of Tom Holland

In 2016, the pair, who are now 27, were just 19 years old. “We were both very, very young, but my career was already kind of going, and his changed overnight,” Zendaya told Vogue. “One day you’re a kid, and you’re at the pub with your friends, and then the next day you’re Spider-Man.

“I definitely watched his life kind of change in front of him. But he handled it really beautifully,” the Hollywood star gushed.

Holland is starring in a new London production of Romeo & Juliet as Zendaya tells the publication she “could not be more proud,” and was going to try and see him at as many shows as she possibly can.

The Challengers star also opened up about the ‘pressures’ of a public relationship, but they don’t let it get to them, reminiscing on their visit to the Louvre in 2022, which they were advised against visiting, however, they went ahead.

“It was actually fine,” the star told Vogue. “You just kind of get used to the fact that, ‘Oh, I’m also one of these art pieces you’re going to take a picture of.’ I just gotta be totally cool with it and just live my life.”

The star stuns in another bridal look

With a Zendaya interview, comes another stunning look, and one which definitely stood out was her ‘bridal’ look.

The star wore a custom Schiaparelli Haute Couture dress, along with a dramatic white veil, and silver statement jewelry.

It’s not the first time the star has recently sported a ‘bridal look’. In fact, just last week, she stunned in Paris in an all-white dress.

The optimist in us is hoping she’s throwing out some hints regarding her future with Tom, but the reality is, that she looks stunning in white, as she does in anything.
