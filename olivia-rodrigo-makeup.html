Her 18 Fave Products &amp; Best Looks

Olivia Rodrigo might be Gen Z’s fashion icon—but her hair and makeup looks have quickly become just as popular for inspiration. While other celebrities are on top of every makeup trend, Olivia’s beauty routine remains simple and classic. Her makeup staples are winged liner and a red lip—a combo that will never go out of style. If you’re in search of a timeless makeup and hair routine, take note of Olivia Rodrigo’s favorite beauty products.

As a huge Glossier fan, Olivia teamed up with the brand in 2022 to release her own limited-edition products. Like Olivia’s signature makeup look, Glossier’s products focus on enhancing your natural beauty. Olivia Rodrigo and Glossier go together like peanut butter and jelly, so it’s no wonder Olivia uses so many of the brand’s products regularly.

While Olivia’s usual makeup is simple, her glittery Guts Tour looks are a slightly elevated version of her usual routine. To complement her sparkly ensembles, she wears chunky silver or purple eye glitter with confetti stars to finish the look. While this is a change from her everyday look, her iconic winged liner and red lip remain staples.

From Glossier’s Ultralip to The Ordinary’s Niacinamide and Zinc Serum, Olivia’s favorite products are surprisingly easy to use and at a reasonable price range. No matter which of Olivia’s beauty looks you take inspiration from, just know there’s tons of great products to try and add to your personal holy grail.

  • Olivia Rodrigo's Favorite Hair Care Products

    Olivia is known to give us amazing hair looks. If you’re hair’s looking a little flat, try out Olivia’s faves to serve your own hair looks.

    Olly Women’s Multivitamin Gummy

    As the first step of her beauty routine, Olivia takes an Olly Women’s Multivitamin Gummy. Besides liking the taste, she’s noticed a big difference in her hair since she started taking them.

  • Olivia Rodrigo's Favorite Skin Care Products

    How does Olivia keep her skin looking flawless with such a hectic schedule? These are her favorite skincare products that help detox her skin when she’s on the go.

    Epionce Milky Lotion Cleanser

    To cleanse, Olivia uses the Epionce Milky Lotion Cleanser. After dealing with reoccurring breakouts, Olivia said cutting her skincare routine has seriously saved her skin. A recent addition to her skincare routine, Olivia uses a Gua Sha tool to reduce puffiness in her face.

  • Olivia Rodrigo's Favorite Face Products

    Olivia’s all about minimalistic makeup so of course her favorite face products emphasize looking natural. Her picks are all surprisingly easy-to-use too which is perfect for at home application.

    Glossier Perfecting Skin Tint

    Glossier’s Perfecting Skin Tint is the perfect everyday foundation. It’s lightweight and natural, which is exactly Olivia’s vibe. Apply moisturizer or a primer and follow with this for a flawless base.

    Glossier Stretch Concealer

    To cover stubborn spots, Olivia uses Stretch Concealer for more coverage. The dewy finish makes it ideal for natural-looking skin.

    Glossier Cloud Paint

    Cloud Paint is an easy-to-use liquid blush. Olivia mixed shades Beam and Storm for her 2022 Grammys look.

    Glossier Futuredew

    Olivia’s makeup artist uses Futuredew to highlight the high points of her face.

  • Olivia Rodrigo's Favorite Eye Makeup Products

    Olivia’s signature winged liner is seriously inspirational. Thankfully we found her go-to eye products to get the look and our best guess for the glitter she uses on tour.

    Glossier Monochromes Palette

    Olivia’s gorgeous sparkly eyes at the 2022 Grammys are thanks to the Monochromes Palette in Mist. To get the look, apply the matte shades first and then top with the glitter shades.

    Glossier Pro Tip

    No Olivia Rodrigo look is complete without winged eyeliner. For the perfect wing, use Pro Tip.

    Glossier Lash Slick

    To finish off her eyes, Olivia uses Lash Slick mascara for perfectly long lashes.

    Lemonhead Spacepaste Glitter Concentrate

    Olivia’s Guts Tour chunky silver glitter is extremely similar to Lemonhead’s Spacepaste Glitter Concentrate in Silverlake. (Or Violet Hour for her purple looks.) To get her look, apply all over the lid with a brush.

  • Olivia Rodrigo's Favorite Lip Products

    Olivia is always on point with her lip colors. We tracked down her favorite lip products so you too can have perfect all American lips.

    Glossier Ultralip in Villa

    Olivia’s favorite lippie is the Ultralip in Villa. She’s worn it on multiple occasions, both for her everyday routine and on red carpets.
