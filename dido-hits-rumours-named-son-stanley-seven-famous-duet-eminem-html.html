Dido hits back at rumours she named her son Stanley, seven, after her famous duet with Eminem

She was propelled into stardom when she featured on Eminem's hit single Stan back in 2000. 

But Dido slammed speculation she named her seven-year-old son Stanley after the track as she discussed her music comeback on Lorraine on Monday.

The singer, 47, who is married to Rohan Gavin, told host Lorraine Kelly she had picked her son's name when she was a teenager and the rapper's song named after the moniker was purely a coincidence.  

Hitting back: Dido slammed speculation she named her seven-year-old son Stanley after her duet 'Stan' with Eminem during her appearance on Lorraine on Monday

She said: 'When I was a teenager, I was with my mate, I remember sitting on a wall, and they were like, "What are you going to call your kid?" and I was like, “Stanley."

Eminem helped introduce Dido to a US audience when he received permission to sample the first verse of Thank You in his single Stan, which was written about an obsessive fan.

Dido also appeared in the music video as Stan's pregnant girlfriend. Interest soared in her debut album, with No Angel going on to become the top-selling album of 2001 worldwide.

And Dido appeared on the show to mark her comeback to the music business, 15 years after she released her last hit track White Flag in 2003. 

The singer discussed her upcoming tour, but her youthful appearance quickly got viewers talking. 

Music breakthrough: Eminem introduced Dido to a US audience with his single Stan. Dido appeared in the music video as Stan's pregnant girlfriend (pictured)

Fans were also quick to swoon over Dido, with one tweeting: 'Dido hasn't aged at all. Such a beautiful lady.' 

Another joked she has the skin of a baby, tweeting: 'She actually looks eight-months-old! Good on her.' 

A third fan praised: 'Wonderful to see Dido on @reallorraine this morning, such a lovely down to earth lady and a gifted artist, pleased she has a new album coming out.' 

Even Lorraine noticed and asked Dido her 'secret' to looking so young, but Dido simply shied away from the answer. 

Return: Dido appeared on the show to mark her comeback to the music business 15 years after she released her last hit track White Flag in 2003 

Ageless beauty! Dido pictured on stage at The Eden Project in Cornwall in July 2005. She's about to go on tour again and has barely aged since she was last riding high in the charts 

The giggling singer replied: 'I don't know.'

However, the star has admitted she has taken time out in between her albums to enjoy quality time with her son, Stanley. 

She said: 'I do a lot of living between albums, and then also had my son, and I want to just hang out with him.'  

Next venture: The singer said she was feeling nervous but excited about her return to the music scene and her upcoming tour

 

Youthful: Fans were quick to praise the star for her glowing skin and youthful appearance, with one viewer likening her to a newborn baby on Twitter 

Explaining why she had taken a break from releasing music, Dido previously admitted she struggled to write good songs after welcoming her first child Stanley with husband Rohan in 2011.

She explained on BBC Radio 2 last week: 'After I had my son I definitely have a couple of years where most what I wrote wasn't very good.

'I actually knew it at the time, when I write songs there's always a bit conflicted, but then when I had a kid I thought ''I'm quite happy.''

The star will also be embarking on her first world tour in 15 years, as she confessed the previous two albums did not seem to fit with the live shows.
