Lil Waynes The Dedication Tour

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Lil Wayne announced The Dedication Tour. The Dedication Tour is a personal thank you from Lil Wayne dedicated to all of his fans in cities he has rarely visited on his past tour runs. The tour, produced by Live Nation in association with Al Haymon Events, kicks off on Jan. 21 at the El Paso County Coliseum in El Paso, Tex. and will visit cities across the United States. Rae Sremmurd will be special guests on The Dedication Tour.
