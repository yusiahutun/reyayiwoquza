James Gang? | Local News

Four arrested on variety of charges; Windemere burglary items recovered

A glance at the Jones County jail docket makes it appear that there was a roundup in the Wild West on Tuesday. Four suspects with “James” as part of their name were picked up in consecutive arrests on unrelated charges during a 10-hour period. All are white men in their 30s.
