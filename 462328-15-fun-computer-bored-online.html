15 fun things to do on the computer when you're bored online

An idle mind is said to be the devil's workshop, and that's why many people strive to have something to do even in their free time. While some engaged in outdoor activities in the past, technology has made it possible to engage in exciting activities indoors. A good number of people have gym spaces in their homes and see working out as the perfect way to kill boredom. Be it as it may, some don't want to lift a muscle in their leisure time hence the need for fun things to do on the computer when you're bored.

There is a whole world of activities one can do over the internet, yet sometimes people cannot find fun things to do when bored. Fun things to do on the computer when you’re bored

Software and application developers are working day and night to ensure you have fun things to do online with friends. Traditionally, there were a few computer games like checkers, chess, FIFA, and the Need for Speed, but the situation is quite different today. Below are some of the fun things you can do online:

1. Play video games

Video games top the list of fun things to do online with friends. Most games have the options of single-player and multiple players; hence it is possible to play by yourself or with friends and strangers. For these, you have to visit websites that host the games and select things like the game level and the type of opponents you wish to play with.

Online video games serve more than just as activities to pass the time. Through these, you can interact with gamers from other parts of the world and make acquaintances. While professional gamers probably need gaming computers and special software, others can rely on browser-based games that are equally fun.

2. Watch YouTube videos

YouTube is one of the most popular online platforms and the go-to place for bored people. Here, you will come across an endless supply of interesting videos to watch. You will find music, films, documentaries, and animation, among many other things.

If you have a YouTube channel, you can upload videos of your own and share them with the rest of the world. This will make you a content creator, and if you monetize your account, you can earn money while doing what you enjoy, which is why it tops the fun things to do on a laptop when you are bored.

3. Read articles on the web

This is a fun activity for people who can describe themselves as avid readers. The internet is like one huge library; there are probably billions of articles on almost every sphere of life, whether academic, fiction, comic or where your interest might be.

Finding articles online is an easy task. One of the most efficient ways is through the use of search engines. Key in keywords of the topics you wish to explore, and you will be bombarded with numerous results.

4. Exploring new music

Some soothing music is all you need to lighten your mood when you are bored. The internet is a good place to discover new music you have never heard before. In addition, you will get the opportunity to sample unique and authentic music from different areas of the world.

You can take this activity a notch higher by entering singing competitions. You can do it as a team with friends or alone. This can be done for amusement or with the hope of winning prize money.

5. Play online slots

Playing online slots is a fun way of spending leisure time over the internet. Many websites host slot machines that come with a variety of themes. The most popular online slot themes are classic games, but they are a perfect place to be for an adrenaline rush.

6. Taking virtual tours

Perhaps you would love to visit exotic places in faraway places in the world, but you don't have the resources to do so. Fortunately, your dream can come true, only that you won't physically visit those places, but you will be doing so online.

Many websites and organizations, such as corporations and even cities, offer virtual tours to interested individuals. It is therefore upon you to choose the scenic places to visit and see what other parts of the world have to offer.

7. Exploring e-books

Articles are usually short, but if you need lengthy literary works to read, then eBooks might be the better option. Several websites offer free eBooks, which you can download or read online. In addition, other websites sell eBooks at a small fee used to pay the authors of the books.

8. Doing animations

Learning a new skill is not the worst way to spend your free time because you might discover your hidden talent. Luckily for you, you don't have to learn coding to be able to create short animations. There are many software and tools which you can use to make your animations.

Many websites have free and paid-for tools that cater to various needs. In addition, since this is one of the creative things to do on your computer, sometimes there are contests in which winners are awarded prizes.

9. Listening to podcasts

A podcast is a series of audio episodes focused on a particular topic or theme. But, of course, music is not the only thing you can listen to online. There are podcasts focused on diverse things ranging from sports to business and things like religion.

10. Exploring social media platforms

There are limitless social media platforms that are exciting to explore. The platforms are meant to connect people be it family members, friends or even strangers. However, such platforms have evolved into sources of news as well as income.

Social media apps like TikTok come with many tools for editing photos and creating videos. With such tools, you will never lack a fun activity to do. For example, you can easily create a collage of your favourite photos for other family members to see.

11. Participate in online discussions

If you are passionate about a topic, there are many forums where you can discuss it. For instance, there are online chat rooms with themes to which you can contribute. The good thing about online discussions is that you interact with a global community.

12. Try online dating

If boredom is killing you and you happen to be single, then finding a date wouldn’t be a bad idea. There are tons of websites and apps that specialize in matchmaking. Here, you will find thousands of photos of potential suitors, and all you have to do is select the one you like.

Many people, including celebrities, have met their partners on online dating sites. Most of these sites offer you the option of serious relationships or friendships. Either way, it is a fun activity to engage in if you are bored online.

13. Shop for new things

Since everything is digital, you no longer have to leave your house to go shopping. Instead, you can easily shop for items from overseas from the comfort of your home. In addition, many big corporations have online retailers through which their products can be purchased.

14. Start a blog

Starting a blog is one of the cool things to learn on a computer and probably the most productive way you can spend your free time. It could also be a journal in which you record all the interesting activities in your life. Writing and keeping a journal is not just for remembrance, but it also has other benefits, including psychological and mental.

There are lots of websites that provide free tools for writing online journals and blogs. However, since it is not something you do once but regularly, the best platforms are apps. This habit keeps you occupied and helps develop your writing skills.

15. Take online tests and quizzes

By taking online tests and quizzes, you will not only be learning new things, but you will also know more about yourself. They can be anything ranging from professional tests and IQ tests to questionnaires and surveys.

If you want to know more about who you are, then online quizzes and tests are the things to do on the computer when bored at work. Apart from being exciting, the results can be useful in future employment when hiring companies want to know more about your personality.
