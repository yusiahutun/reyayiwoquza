CS Rebecca Miano's Son Weds Lover in Flashy Traditional Wedding, Politicians Steal Show at Event

  • Cabinet Secretaries, among them Aisha Jumwa, Susan Nakhumicha and Florence Bore, stunned in stylish outfits while attending CS Rebecca Miano's son's wedding
  • Peter Miano and his lover, Wambui Kibe, tied the knot in a colourful, star-studded traditional wedding on April 6
  • The lovebirds wore matching brown outfits worn by couples during Kikuyu traditional weddings

April seems to be the new preferred wedding month for Kenyan lovebirds of late, and over the weekend, several weddings went down.

How Aisha Jumwa dressed to CS Rebecca Miano's son's wedding

Trade Cabinet Secretary Rebecca Miano and her family threw a flashy ceremony for their son, Peter Miano.

Miano and his lover, Wambui Kibe, tied the knot in a colourful traditional wedding on April 6. The wedding was attended by several politicians, among them cabinet secretaries.

Miani and Wambui wore matching brown outfits worn by couples during Kikuyu traditional weddings.

The CSs, including Aisha Jumwa, Susan Nakhumicha, Florence Bore, Soipan Tuya, Mercy Wanjau, Moses Kuria, Rebecca Miano and other politicians, wore stylish long kitenge dresses to the wedding, and each stood out with their fashion.

CS Bore shared photos from the wedding and captioned:

Celebrated love with family and friends of Peter Miano and Wambui Kibe, a beautiful couple who complement each other in admirable ways. Delighted to share this day with my friend and colleague CS Rebecca Miano at her son’s ruracio in Nairobi. I wish the couple a lifetime of joy and God’s Blessings!"
