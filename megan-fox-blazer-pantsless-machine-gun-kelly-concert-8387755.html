Megan Fox Left Her Pants at Home For Machine Gun Kellys Concert in Brazil

If one thing is for certain, it's that Megan Fox will always deliver a high-fashion twist on concert-goer attire while proving she’s Machine Gun Kelly's number one fan. During MGK's (born Colson Baker) latest concert, Fox showed her support while simultaneously pulling off an elevated take on the pantsless trend.

On Saturday, the actress was photographed supporting her fiancé during GP Week in Brazil at Allianz Parque. While we could hardly see past Megan’s fiery red bob that she first debuted on a date night with MGK, her outfit refused to be ignored. For the occasion, she left her pants at home and opted for an oversized double-breasted beige blazer with nothing but a plunging black bra underneath. A pair of red-soled PVC sandals, matching cat-eye sunglasses, and a coordinating manicure provided the finishing touches to her concert-ready look.

Her shoulder-length bob was styled in subtle beach waves and a middle part, and she complemented her bold brows with pink glossy lips and rosy cheeks.

Judging by Megan's latest outings, she's loving a good blazer moment as much as we do. Earlier this fall, the actress sported a similar silhouette with a pair of thigh-high lace-up snakeskin boots when stepping out with MGK during their visit to the Big Apple. Prior to that, she opted for a deconstructed blazer dress that featured massive ab-baring cutouts on each side and an open circular detail at her sternum.
