Dunkin' fans spot odd detail in new Ice Spice collaboration ingredient list as they ask 'people drin

DUNKIN' fans have noticed an odd detail in the new collaboration with Ice Spice after taking a closer look at the ingredient list.

A photo of the ingredients in the Ice Spice Munchkins Drink was shared on X, formerly Twitter, and several took shots at the ridiculousness of it.

The drink features pumpkin munchkins, the name Dunkin' uses for its doughnut hole treats, coffee syrup, liquid cane sugar, and cream, topped with a caramel drizzle.

The size of the drink will determine how many pumps of each ingredient are inside.

"Where is the coffee?" asked one X user.

"Who needs that much sugar?" asked another person. "Especially when the drink includes caramel."

A third person wrote: "How you going to put 2-4 blended munchkins in a cup that is completely full of ice already?"

There seemed to be confusion as to what Dunkin' meant by "coffee syrup" and whether or not the drink actually contained coffee.

"Coffee SYRUP? Not even like a shot of espresso. This drink is quite literally straight sugar in different outfits and ice Jesus Christ," wrote another person.

A fifth asked: "Is coffee syrup actual coffee or does this drink just not contain coffee?"

"People drink this? Willingly?" asked another person.

The drink was made available on Wednesday, September 13, and references the rapper's hit song Munch (Feelin' U) and is a limited-time-only latte as a part of Dunkin's fall menu for the year.

After October 31, 2023, the drink will no longer be available.

Ice Spice, whose real name is Isis Gaston, promoted the drink on X, writing: "America runs on MUNCHKINS."

The post featured a photo of a Dunkin' munchkin wearing Ice's signature Princess gold necklace.

While some were wary of buying the drink, others were excited to try it.

"I will be buying Dunkin more often now that you got a brand deal with them," wrote one fan.

"I speak for everyone when I say we WILL be munching," wrote another.

A third wrote: "OH WE WILL BE EATING ALL OF THE MUNCHKINS."
