The biopic of her acting career with TLC named CrazySexyCool: The TLC Story, was aired on VH1 in 2013. 

Married/ Husband

Tionne Watkins' love life is no secret. She was married to a fellow rapper Mack 10, whose real name is Dedrick D-Mon Rolison. The ex-couple stayed in a romantic relationship and took their bond to the next level with a marital tie. They shared a wedding vows in August of 2000 and savored their married life for four years.
