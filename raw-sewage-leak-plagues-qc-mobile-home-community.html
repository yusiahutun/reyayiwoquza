Raw sewage leak plagues QC mobile home community WHBF OurQuadCities.com

Complaints about a raw sewage leak at a Quad-City mobile home community got the attention of investigators with Iowa's Department of Natural Resources. Local 4 News visited the property and saw large puddles between two of the homes and smelled some sewage. For more on this story, visit our website, OurQuadCities.com.
