Photos Of The Award-Winning Actress Hollywood Life

See photos of iconic British actress Helen Mirren at red carpet events, award shows, and more Hollywood events.

Helen Mirren is a legend. The U.K. native is without a doubt one of the most talented actresses to live. Some of her most notable film roles include ‘The Queen,’ ‘The Audience,’ ‘Woman in Gold,’ ‘Hitchcock,’ and the ‘Red’ and ‘Fast &amp; Furious’ movie franchises. On the small screen, she’s starred in the hit British TV series ‘Prime Suspect’ as Detective Chief Inspector Jane Tennison. For her hard work in acting, Helen has been nominated for 4 Oscars, winning one. She’s also won a British Academy Film Award, a Golden Globe Award, and four Emmy Awards.

Outside of her career, Helen has been married to film director Taylor Hackford since 1997. They have no children together. In 2017, Helen officially became a U.S. citizen. To see photos of Helen, click through the gallery.
