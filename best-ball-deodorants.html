Bolster Your Comfort &amp; Confidence

Men’s grooming used to be pretty standard, especially back when your grandfather was on the hunt for men’s first date outfits and smell-good products. Aftershave, cologne, deodorant, shaving cream… that was pretty much it. Times have changed, and there are now more men’s grooming brands than you can shake a stick at. Today, it’s honestly required to look after your appearance beyond a simple splash of cold water on your face every morning. That extends below the belt, too, which is why you need ball deodorant or ball spray.

*Record scratch* Wait, what? Yes, below-the-belt care is big business, and that’s where it gets even more specific. Ball spray. Ball deodorant. All things ball… refreshers. Deodorant specifically for your nether regions exists, something that was largely unheard of even a decade ago. 

Why Have Ball Deodorants & Ball Sprays Become So Popular? 

Companies like MANSCAPED were founded with a highly specific focus on just that area. It’s a brave new world, but one that can bolster your confidence, your hygiene (of course), and perhaps even your *ahem* performance. (These tips on how to stop overthinking and be more present during sex can certainly help.) After all, you’ll want to look good and feel good to play good.

“This shift in behavior can be largely attributed to changing societal norms around masculinity and self-care, and the breaking of longstanding taboos,” says MANSCAPED Chief Marketing Officer Marcelo Kertész.

While it might not be the first thing you talk about with your partner or certainly your buddies, it’s almost expected that your grooming routine in that area is on par with the rest of your routine overall. Kertész calls the category “desirable, popular, and aspirational for men,” and he notes that the company has focused on humor and community engagement “to help make these grooming practices a shared and open topic among men.”

When you think about it, it’s kind of a funny premise on the surface, but one that shouldn’t be overlooked. Add another step to your grooming routine in an area you might not have considered, and shop these picks for the best ball deodorants and ball sprays for men.
