Say Goodbye to Sarah Jessica Parkers Long Curls the Actress Now Has Short Hair!

Bye bye, long curls!

Sex and the City star Sarah Jessica Parker just debuted her new short hair for the first time on Wednesday, July 12 — and we’re loving her updated look, although, it’s unclear if it’s real or if it’s a wig.

The actress presumably snipped her longer tresses into a near-bob for an upcoming role in the new romantic drama, Best Day of My Life. And by the looks of the new photos of Sarah Jessica on set in NYC, she even went blonder this time around!

Sarah Jessica in July 2017.

We wonder how her husband, Matthew Broderick, feels about her new tresses. In a recent exclusive interview with Closer Weekly, he gushed about his wife. “I feel very lucky,” he said, adding that she’s “beautiful” and “hilarious. “She’s a great person.”

Over the past few years, the 52-year-old sported long brown hair with blonde highlights for her role in the HBO series Divorce. During her SATC days, she typically donned long blond strands as Carrie Bradshaw.

Sarah Jessica will star alongside Renée Zellweger, Taylor Kinney, and more in the drama Best Day of My Life as a jazz vocalist whose life gets turned upside down after she receives some unfortunate health news.

And maybe, just maybe, she’s going to be busy soon filming a third installment of the popular Sex and the City franchise! Earlier this year, she opened up about the possibility of another movie based off her hit show.

“It’s in a warming drawer. It’s never been a ‘no’… it’s always been an ‘if’ and ‘when,'” she told Marie Claire in February. “And that remains the question being asked by two very thoughtful people who are taking into very serious consideration those people who devoted a lot of time. We’re not cavalier about the way we arrive at that decision.”
