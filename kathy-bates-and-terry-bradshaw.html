About

Kathy Bates is a 75 year old American Actress. Born Kathleen Doyle Bates on 28th June, 1948 in Memphis, Tennessee, USA, she is famous for her roles as Annie Wilkes in the 1990 psychological thriller film Misery, Evelyn Couch in the 1991 comedy-drama film Fried Green Tomatoes, Dolores Claiborne in the 1995 psychological thriller drama film Dolores Claiborne, "the Unsinkable" Molly Brown in the 1997 epic romance and disaster film Titanic, Libby Holden in the 1998 comedy-drama film Primary Colors, Helen "Mama" Boucher in the 1998 sports comedy film The Waterboy, Roberta Hertzel in the 2002 comedy-drama film About Schmidt, Queen Victoria in the 2004 action adventure comedy film Around the World in 80 Days, Harriet Korn in the NBC legal drama series Harry's Law, Delphine LaLaurie, Ethel Darling, Iris, Agnes Mary Winstead, Thomasin White, and Ms. Miriam Mead in the award-winning FX anthology horror series American Horror Story, and Bobi Jewell in the 2019 biographical drama film Richard Jewell in a career that spans 1963–present and 1970–present. Her zodiac sign is Cancer.

Terry Bradshaw is a 75 year old American Football (American) Player. Born Terry Paxton Bradshaw on 2nd September, 1948 in Shreveport, Louisiana, United States, he is famous for Sportscaster, former Steelers quarterback. His zodiac sign is Virgo.
