Keith Apicary Biography, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Keith Apicary is a multi-talented personality, who works as an American professional actor, comedian, dancer, YouTuber, filmmaker, and also stunt performer. He has gained a huge appreciation for his appearance on ‘Comedy Central’ and ‘Adult Swim’. He caught the media’s attention when he participated in the famous reality TV show ‘America’s Got Talent as a contestant.

Early Life

Keith Apicary whose real name is Nathan James Barnatt was born on 2nd February 1981 in Milford, Massachusetts, United States. He was born to his mother, Maryellen Barnatt, and his father, Thomas Barnatt, and grew up in his hometown alongside his sibling brothers, Joshua, who is a freelance photographer, and Seth Barnatt, who is a writer and is best known for his collaborative work with his brother.

Nathan went to attend ‘Medway High School’ and graduated in 1999. After his graduation, he joined ‘Uprights Citizens Brigade’, where he studied Improv and Sketch. He also took training under UMASS Boston Theatre. He is probably well educated and is qualified for being an amazing actor, which one can experience in his fantastic movies.

Keith Apicary Career

The legend actor, Nathan initiated his professional acting career, when he started working at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre in Los Angeles. He was represented by ‘Carolina Talent Agency’ for his acting at first. He started from working at commercials to movies to Tv shows. Some of his best works include, ‘Under Belly, where he appeared with his alter ego Keith Apicary, High School USA, Comedy Band! Bang! Kirby Buckets, Adam Ruins Everything, Talking Classics, and Dad.
