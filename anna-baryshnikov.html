Early Life

On the 22nd of May 1992, in New York City, New York, the United States of America, Anna Baryshnikov was born. As of 2023, she is 31 years old and holds American nationality.

In addition, she has her belief in the Christian religion and has a birth sign of Gemini. Similarly, she belongs to the Russian-American ethnicity.

Anna is the daughter of Mikhail Baryshnikov (father) and Lisa Rinehart (mother). In addition, her father is a dancer, and choreographer while her mother is a dancer, writer, video journalist, and director by profession.

Similarly, she has two siblings and one half-sibling. Peter Andrew Baryshnikov is her elder brother who is a photographer by profession while her younger sister’s name is Sofia-Luisa Baryshnikova, who is a ballet dancer.

In addition, her elder half-sister’s name is Shura Baryshnikov and is an actress, dancer, dance educator, and choreographer by profession.

He appeared in two television series ‘Doll & Em’ portraying the role of a Journalist and ‘Blue Bloods’ as Jenny Strong in the year 2015.
