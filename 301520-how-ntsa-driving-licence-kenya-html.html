NTSA new driving licence requirements

Getting a DL is easy provided that you meet the following conditions.

  • At least sixteen years for motorcycles
  • At least eighteen years for a personal vehicle
  • At least twenty four years with four years of experience for matatus and omnibuses
  • Pass the competency test drive
  • Not suffering from a disease or physical disability that can hinder you when on the steering wheel
  • Can read a vehicle identification plate from a distance of twenty-five metres
  • Can read and write in English and Swahili
  • Has no record of traffic offences
  • Has no criminal record
  • Has the police clearance certificate

This NTSA new driving licence application process only requires your physical presence when taking your lessons at your school of choice when taking the competency test. The other steps like applying and making payments are possible from the comfort of your home.

Here is the process.

  • Create or log into your eCitizen account
  • Choose the National Transport and Safety Authority option
  • Click on Make Application
  • Choose Driving Licenses
  • Click on Provisional Driving License
  • On the next page, fill in your details and the details of your training school
  • Pay Kshs. 650 and print your Provisional DL

Follow the same process if you have the old DL and you want to get the new one.

How do I renew my NTSA driving licence?

For the renewal of driving licence, you do not have to visit any office to get a new DL. The renewal only requires a few clicks, and you will be done.

  • Log into your eCitizen account
  • Choose the National Transport and Safety Authority option
  • Click on Make Application
  • Choose Driving Licenses
  • Choose the one-year or three-year license renewal option
  • Pay Kshs. 1,450 and you will be good to go.
