Molly Sims Ties the Knot to Movie Producer

Molly Sims is officially off the market.

The sexy 38-year-old model/actress and movie producer Scott Stuber got married Saturday at a vineyard in Napa Valley -- and there were a couple celebs in the crowd.

Fergie and Josh Duhamel, who co-starred with Sims on the TV show "Las Vegas," were guests of the bride. Vince Vaughn, who starred in Stuber-produced flick "The Break Up," was also reportedly present.

Molly wore a gown from Marchesa and Lorraine Schwartz jewels for the ceremony.

Sims and Stuber got engaged in May after dating for a year and a half.
