Maple Eased Edge Butcher Block Laurel Mercantile

  • Made in Laurel, Mississippi
  • American Hardwood
  • Eased edge finish
  • Oil finished & food safe
  • Available in three sizes: 
    • Small - 9.5″ x 12″ x 1.5″ 
    • Medium - 12″ x 16″ x 1.5″
