Who is Jenni Rivera?

Jenni Rivera was a famous American singer, songwriter, actress, television producer, spokesperson, philanthropist, and entrepreneur. Her particular styles of music included Mariachi, Banda, and Norteno.

She began her music career as a recording artist back in 1992. Though her career did not take off immediately, she kept working and eventually attained fame, which was rightly deserved. Unfortunately, she died on December 8th, 2012.
