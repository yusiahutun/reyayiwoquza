Taylor Swift's dad could make things quite difficult for her and Travis Kelce

In an ideal world, Travis Kelce would want the Swift family to become Chiefs fans, but Taylor Swift’s dad may not make it easy.

Taylor Swift and Travis have been swiftly moving through epic milestones in their relationship (pardon the pun). The celebrity couple have already met each other’s parents, and the NFL heartthrob tried to make the impossible possible when he met Tay Tay’s dad, Scott. Travis Kelce gifted Scott a Chiefs lanyard, but the star’s father is a hardcore fan of the Eagles.

Taylor Swift’s dad and his favorite team could make things ‘difficult’

Taylor’s dad Scott is a big Eagles fan, and her new beau has sworn to turn him into a Chiefs supporter. Travis Kelce’s charm is already working on Scott as the NFL celebrity gloats about how he persuaded him to wear a Chiefs lanyard.

“Got ‘em all over here to the good side, baby,” Travis shares with brother Jason in the latest episode of the New Heights podcast.

But the quest to win Scott over will not be easy for Travis, as his brother Jason plays for the Philadelphia Eagles and is now ready to defend his team.

Jason sent a message to Scott through the podcast saying: “You’re gonna let this man’s devilishly good looks and relationship with your daughter sway you from a lifetime of fandom, Scott? This is ridiculous.”

The adorable bond between Scott and Travis

Taylor Swift has dated a number of Hollywood heartthrobs, but fans are quite amazed to see how well Travis and Scott are getting along. They appear to have a bond of their own, which is refreshing to watch as it unfolds in front of fans’ eyes.

As Taylor and Travis move on to a more serious relationship, the duo are preparing for their parents to meet each other. If the interactions between Trav and Scott are anything to go by, it’ is working like it’ll be magic! After Taylor impressed both Donna and Ed, Travis also got quite close to Scott on the Argentina tour.

Videos captured by fans show Scott’s excitement when Taylor changed her Karma lyrics to “Karma is the guy on the Chiefs, coming straight home to me,” who even reaches out to Travis for a high five.

Another video reveals Scott is happily capturing Travis lip-syncing to the Anti-Hero crooner’s lyrics.
