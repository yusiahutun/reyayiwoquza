Andrew Barth Feldman is an American actor, singer, and producer. Andrew Barth Feldman was the winner of the 2018 BroadwayCon Lip Sync Battle.

Early Life

Andrew Barth Feldman was born on May 7th, 2002. Presently, he is 21 years old and his sun sign is Taurus. He is from Manhasset, New York, USA. His parents are David N. Feldman and Barbara Barth, who passed away in August 201919. His mother was a school administrator and his father was a lawyer.

Moreover, he grew up with an older sister and his cousins who he refers them as his brothers, Scott and Matt. Andrew went to Lawrence Woodmere Academy in New York. Later, he studied at Lawrence Woodmere Academy.

Career

Andrew went on to star in many local and school productions, including Annie, Grease, and Rent. He created a YouTube channel in high school to do projects for school. This personality began his acting career in musical theatre by participating in local productions as a child. Andrew won a Jimmy Award for his high school musical Catch Me If You Can in 2018.

In 2019, he starred in the musical Dear Evan Hansen on Broadway. Feldman also guest-starred in the television musical series High School Musical: The Musical: The Series in 2021. Similarly, he started a fundraiser for the Actors Fund called Broadway Jackbox in March 2020. Also, he created a murder mystery series called Broadway Whodunit. He founded his own theatre company, Zneefrock Productions, to raise money for autism awareness.

Likewise, on February 11, 2021, it was announced that Feldman would make his television acting debut in the Disney+ show, High School Musical: The Musical: The Series. Moreover, on May 7, 2021, Andrew appeared in the short film Thespians, Casey Likes, a 2019 Jimmy Award finalist. He made his film acting debut in April 2023 in the Netflix film A Tourist’s Guide to Love. Further, this personality starred alongside Jennifer Lawrence in No Hard Feelings in June 2023.
