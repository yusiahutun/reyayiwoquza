Ezekiel Lewis Promoted to President of Epic Records

published on March 22, 2024
NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Ezekiel Lewis has been promoted to President of Epic Records. He will continue to report to Epic Records’ Chairwomen and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Sylvia Rhone. Lewis will lead the A&R division while helping run the day-to-day operations of the Sony Music Group-owned label. Lewis has served as the company’s Executive Vice President (EVP) and Head of A&R since 2020.
