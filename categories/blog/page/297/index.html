Playa Bowls to Open in Stuart

published on May 15, 2024
Playa Bowls will now call Stuart home. The brand will unveil its newest location (1925 NW Federal Highway) with a kick-off celebration May 20, featuring free bowls and limited-edition t-shirts for the first 50 guests in line, along with other giveaways and entertainment provided throughout the day.  The Stuart shop is owned and operated by franchise owners Michael and Jenna Zorn. With plans to open a second Playa Bowls location later this summer in Jupiter, the husband-and-wife duo saw an opportunity to grow their passion for the nutritious bowl concept and expand Playa Bowls’ footprint in Florida.
