Marshall Tucker, Namesake of the Marshall Tucker Band, Dead at 99

published on March 05, 2024
The Marshall Tucker Band are mourning the loss of the man who lent their group his name. Marshall Tucker, a blind piano tuner who hailed from South Carolina, died on Friday (Jan. 20) at the age of 99. The Southern rock group — founded by Toy Caldwell, Doug Gray, Jerry Eubanks, George McCorkle, Paul Riddle and Tommy Caldwell — shared a remembrance of Tucker on social media after learning of his death. Psoriasis types, causes and treatments

published on March 05, 2024
The immune-mediated, non-contagious, inflammatory skin condition psoriasis is relatively common – affecting one in 50 people, and manifests itself as patches of pink plaques with silvery scales on any area of the skin including the scalp and joints. Some may even find that it affects their nails, causing brittleness, discolouration, dents and thickening. Beyond that, psoriasis can also have a knock-on effect on self-esteem. In a similar vein to acne and rosacea, there’s no permanent cure for psoriasis, but there are various treatment options and ways to manage the symptoms – as leading dermatologists share here.
