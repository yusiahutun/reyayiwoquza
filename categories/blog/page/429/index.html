Kenny Chesney, Noise

published on March 12, 2024
Kenny Chesney says “Noise” is his first song with real social importance, and the timing couldn’t be better. Without being political, he takes on politics. Without sounding like a luddite, he challenges technology. Without smelling curmudgeonly, he recalls how things once were. There’s no doubting the urgency in Chesney’s voice as he sings this country-rocker about the constant hum that bears down on us every day.
