Victoria's Secret model Adriana Lima is pregnant.

The 30-year-old Brazilian model and her husband, former NBA star Marko Jaric, are 'very excited' about welcoming their second child into the world later this year.

Adriana - who has a two-year-old daughter Valentina with Marko - told People: 'The entire family is very excited, especially Valentina.

Happy news: Victoria's Secret model Adriana Lima is pregnant with her second child, seen here with husband Marko Jaric back in 2009

'She keeps blowing kisses to my belly and saying, "Kisses for the baby."'

The Victoria's Secret beauty - who met Marko in 2006 and married him in February 2009 - has previously proclaimed motherhood to be the 'best job in the world'.

She has said: 'The best job in the world for me is being a mom. I never thought I could love so much. I always thought, does unconditional love exist? Now I know it does because I feel it.'

Expanding her brood: The Brazilian model already has a daughter, Valentina, with former NBA star Jaric

Brazilian Adriana - who is a devout Roman Catholic - previously admitted she knew she wanted to settle down with Marko and have children when they first met.

She said: 'I knew he was the one and I could see myself married to him and with a big family.'

Back in November Adriana controversially went on a liquid diet, nine days prior to her turn at the annual Victoria's Secret Fashion Show in New York.

Changing shapes: Pictured in September 2009 while pregnant with her first child and, right, back in model for for the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show last November in NYC

The brunette beauty strutted her stuff in an array of sexy outfits which showed off her sculpted figure.

In 2007 and 2008, Lima was ranked as the fourth highest paid model in the world, with an income of around $6m, according to Forbes magazine.

Her Victoria's Secret friend Alessandra Ambrosio is currently expecting he second child with husband Jamie Mazur. They pair already have a three-year-old daughter Anja.

And last year another member of the VS clan, Australian beauty Miranda Kerr, gave birth to her first child, a son Flynn.
