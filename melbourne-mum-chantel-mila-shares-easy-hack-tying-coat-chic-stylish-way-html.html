Melbourne mum Chantel Mila shares easy hack for tying coat in chic and stylish way

A stylish mum-of-two of has revealed the correct way to tie a coat, revealing many Australians are doing it wrong.

Chantel Mila, from Melbourne, shared a video to TikTok wearing a black wrap coat and jeans and said closing the coat and tying it in a simple knot is wrong - it looks much more chic to create a simple loop.

In the clip, she wraps the black belt around her hand several times, creating a small loop before pulling the belt through - which allows her to cinch in the waist much more.

Chantel then shows off the chicly wrapped coat, which looks effortlessly stylish.

'Make any outfit look effortless chic with this simple fold,' she wrote.

Hundreds of people commented on the video, praising Chantel for the easy hack.
