'Obi-Wan Kenobi': Darth Vader's first look in Disney+ series gives fans the chills

Hayden Christensen is all set to reprise his role as Darth Vader in 'Obi-Wan Kenobi' and the second episode gave a first look at the character, leaving fans stunned. The new spin-off marks the third time he has played Anakin Skywalker following on from 'Attack of the Clones' and 'Revenge of the Sith'. Both of those prequel films were directed by 'Star Wars' creator George Lucas. This time around, Deborah Chow gets behind the director's lens.

'Obi-Wan Kenobi' revolves around the aftermath of Skywalker's turn to the Dark Force and the scar it leaves on his teacher (Ewan McGregor returns as Jedi Master Obi). As a quick refresher, the series does give a quick recap before switching to the new storyline. Needless to say, fans were ecstatic after getting their first look at Vader.
