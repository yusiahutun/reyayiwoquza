Jenny McCarthy’s Past affairs and Relationship

Prior settling down with Donnie, Jenny dated number of men who were quite a catch during that time. McCarthy dated manager Ray Manzella from the year 1994 but unfortunately, they called it quit in 1998.

Then, in the same year, McCarthy started dating her former husband John Mallory Asher and got married a year later on September 11. But after few years they separated in September 2005.

The couple shares a son Evan Joseph Asher who was born on May 18, 2002, and later was diagnosed with autism.
