Catherine Zeta-Jones Narrowly Avoids Wardrobe Malfunction In One VERY Sexy Dress

Catherine Zeta-Jones is still one sexy showstopper!

The 46-year-old actress turned heads on Tuesday night at the premiere of her latest film, "Dad's Army," at Leicester Square in London. Despite the rain, the Welsh beauty was the epitome of Hollywood glam in her gorgeous gown at the event.

The stunner highlighted her cleavage in a blue Elie Saab gown that featured a sheer back, side ruching and a high slit that showed off the perfect amount of leg! She styled her hair in glamorous waves and finished the look with metallic sandals, a snake bracelet and diamond earrings.

Getty

While the mom-of-two looked beautiful, she narrowly avoided a wardrobe malfunction after the windy weather caused her dress to blow up on the carpet. Luckily, she was able to hold down the fabric before revealing too much.

The "Chicago" star, took on the role of Rose Winters in the flick, which originally was a beloved British sitcom in the '70s. The star revealed to BBC at the event that the project was very "nostaligic" for her.

"My memories of just wonderful, warm nostalgic of all my family, I think it was on a Saturday night just watching it," she said. "It's such a wonderful heartwarming story it needs the air to breathe on the big screen, it does it beautifully in the film,"she added. "Of course we have an homage to the original series but when you see it, it goes to a different place too. It's a lot of fun!"
