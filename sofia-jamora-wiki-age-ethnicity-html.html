An encounter with a chance changed Sofia Jamora’s life!

Still a teenager, Sofia was shopping for trunks at a show in Westlake when she was spotted by the co-founder of Frankies Bikini. Impressed by her killer looks, the talent scout offered her a modeling gig. And once she accepted the offer, the brunette’s life was never the same.

Well, her success was touched a prominent height when she got featured in two music videos of singer Zayn Malik, Let Entertainer and Me.

Sofia’s Wiki Revealed

The American model, age 21, celebrates her birthday on 6th May. After her chance-start at modeling, Sofia's profile grew immensely. A direct growth was seen on her Instagram's following when her line ValfreXLolli with Lolli Valfre campaign was released.

Right on the path of success, Sofia worked with emerging brands like Oh Polly, Windsor and Tobi. After dabbling into modeling for a while, she finally got signed to Next Models.

Sofia was on cloud nine when she got a chance to work with the world’s leading brand, Forever 21. After her successful campaign with the said brand, she went on to sign with Freedom Models.

Well, her second modeling agency proved to be more fruitful as she got more huge deals while in association with the company. Walking in Miami Swim Week was one of the highlights of her 2017 works.

Sofia’s most significant break came in 2018 when she got featured in Zayn Malik’s music videos. Her resemblance to his on-off girlfriend Gigi Hadid was also the talk of the town when the videos dropped.
