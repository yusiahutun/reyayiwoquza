These Turmeric Copper Cow Coffee Tea Drops Will Boost Your Immunity

With COVID-19, better known as the Coronavirus, running rampant, we’re doing everything we possibly can to keep our immune systems strong and healthy  — including frequent hand washing and other cleanliness measures recommended by the CDC. While other preventative measures, such as taking extra vitamin C, won’t keep you from catching the disease, it certainly can’t hurt to keep our wellness in mind when we’re going about our daily activities, such as drinking tea.

There are plenty of benefits that go hand-in-hand with sipping on your favorite cup, such as reducing cognitive impairment, helping with symptoms that can result from certain conditions, such as diabetes and heart disease, and protecting your teeth and bones. According to reviewers, the combination of the turmeric and the coconut milk tastes like a dream. “This tea has such wonderful taste, and is so simple to make!” wrote one happy shopper.
