Jennifer Lopez channels Taylor Swift as she tells fans her beauty secrets

Nothing screams the festive season more than a red lip, and every beauty-lover has their favorite lipstick in the holiday beauty kit including Jennifer Lopez. The diva is channeling Taylor Swift for her latest red lip tutorial and the Internet is here for it.

Jennifer Lopez is mostly associated with nude lip colors for a perfect red carpet look. The 54-year-old celebrity‘s glowy and bronzed skin, soft smoky understated glam eyes, and glossy nude-pink lips have been her signature style over the years. But J.Lo is switching it up this year for her holiday look and sharing a secret tip to make that red lip pout picture-perfect.

‘Swiftie’ Jennifer Lopez shares tutorial for a perfect red lip look

Taylor Swift and a classic red lip are synonymous, and now even Jennifer Lopez is giving a shoutout to the music legend in her latest beauty tutorial as her red lip inspiration. The movie star, who claims to be a Swiftie, also named Marilyn Monroe and Gwen Stefanie as stars who rock Scarlet Lips daily.

The Selena star shared her secret for a perfect red lip, which lies in the prep. Before applying the red lip color, the singer generally uses a nude lip liner to deepen the lip and make the pout look fuller. Then she uses red lipstick on the middle portion of the lips and takes a lip brush to draw the corners with precision.

Jennifer Lopez’s iconic lip colors over the years

While Jennifer has mostly stayed in the lane of nude lips and glossy brown colors, occasionally the superstar is seen switching it up to some iconic shades, which could be perfect for a glam holiday look.

Brick Orange Glossy lip

The Rebirth crooner was spotted wearing a bright orange glossy lip with a muted off-white dress for a glam look, proving that she can slay any bright lip colors just as well.

Hot Pink

Far from her comfort zone of nude lips, the Anaconda star surprised fans by wearing a hot pink lip this year paired with a floral dress for her Daytime Beauty Awards look, and the Internet loved the gorgeous change.

Dark red

Jennifer’s dark red lips for last Christmas became the talk of beauty town, as it showed how diverse red lips can be for holidays where everyone can put their twist to it.

The singer chose to carry the ox blood lippie with a barely-there lined eye look and a chic bun with pointy high heels and a vibrant sky-blue dress.

With another Christmas right round the corner, we can’t wait to see what look J.Lo opts for this time.
