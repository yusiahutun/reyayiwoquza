Amal Clooney Pairs a Sophisticated Jumpsuit With Cartier Jewelry

Amal and George Clooney’s refined date-night style returned to the spotlight as the couple made a joint appearance at the Prince’s Trust and TKMaxx & Homesense Awards today.

The human-rights lawyer looked sophisticated in a sleek beige jumpsuit. The structured piece included billowing pleated pant legs and a black leather panel wrapped around the waist. She styled the look minimally, simply opting for a pair of classic black pumps, a sand-colored boxy clutch, and white-gold-and-diamond chandelier earrings from Cartier.

Beauty-wise, Amal looked incredible with her dark brown hair worn down in large luscious curls, plus shimmering light pink eye shadow, rosy blush, and a light pink lip.

Meanwhile, the Midnight Sky actor looked suave in a slate gray suit paired with a navy blue collared button-up and glossy black dress shoes. He also wore blue reflective aviator shades to the event.

Getty Images

Amal and George tend not to frequent red carpets, but their rare appearances at high-profile events are always sure to make headlines.

For instance, the couple last stepped out together for the Kennedy Center Honors in December 2022. At the time, the lawyer wore a breathtaking off-the-shoulder gown embellished with sparkling silver sequins from Valentino. She coordinated the silver with her accessories, opting for a glittering silver clutch, diamond drop earrings, and a bracelet cuff. Meanwhile, George opted for a classic black tux.

At the Los Angeles premiere of Ticket to Paradise last October, Amal went the vintage route, opting for a breezy red dress from Alexander McQueen’s Spring 2003 collection. She paired the piece with nude Gianvito Rossi sandals and bronze chandelier earrings. George looked striking, too, wearing a gray suit with a white collared dress shirt.
