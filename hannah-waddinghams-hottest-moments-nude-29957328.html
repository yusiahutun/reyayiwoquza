Hannah Waddingham's hottest moments - nude illusion, bikini display and racy scenes

Eurovision presenter and TV star Hannah Waddingham plays the fierce football club owner in Ted Lasso and isn't afraid of put on a racy display in front of the television cameras

Actress Hannah Waddingham has been wowing fans as the UK's Eurovision host as it takes place in Liverpool and builds up to the grand finale on Saturaday.

She cuts a stylish figure on and off the red carpet as well as having some of the hottest moments in Apple TV+ comedy Ted Lasso as football club owner Rebecca Welton.
