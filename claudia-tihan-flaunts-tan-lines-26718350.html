Claudia Tihan flaunts tan lines in upside down bikini

The upside down bikini trend is back with a bang.

As the year continues to heat up, fashion fans have been sporting their swimwear back to front.

While this may seem like a wardrobe malfunction, there’s method to the madness.

Wearing bikini tops upside down can make your cleavage look more impressive – especially as it exposes the underboob area.

And influencer Claudia Tihan used the craze as an opportunity to flaunt her sexy tan lines for her 1.4 million Instagram followers.

The 25-year-old model, who is from Canada, wore the Fashion Nova Pier Pressure bikini in the trendy way.

Claudia styled her hair into beachy waves and kept her makeup neutral to showcase the clothing.

You can snap up the bikini in the colours "chocolate" or "white".
