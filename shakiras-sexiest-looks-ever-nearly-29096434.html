Shakira is no stranger to a sexy onstage look or two, and she turned up the heat when she performed during VH1 Divas 2002.

The star took to the stage in a skintight black and white pattern catsuit, complete with plunging neckline to the navel that flaunted her washboard abs and ample cleavage.

With her long blonde waves flowing past her shoulders, Shakira finished the smouldering look with smoky eyes for extra sizzle.

Music star

Shakira made a splash in 2002 when she landed a global chart topping hit with the catchy Wherever, Whenever.

The record came with an equally sexy music video, which saw the singer dancing on the beach in a tiny leather crop top and shaking her curves in skintight trousers.

Dripping from head to toe as waves crashed over her, the water was not enough to cool down this sizzling look as Shakira turned up the heat with her dance moves.

Nude illusion

Shakira sent temperatures soaring when she appeared to dance 'naked' in the jaw-dropping video for her hit She Wolf.

The singer wore a nude illusion bodysuit, which seemed to make the singer appear 'naked', topped off with a pair of killer high heels as she writhed around the cage.

The outfit flaunted her peachy booty, stunning curves and toned pins in one of Shakira's hottest ever looks.

80's vibes

Shakira took inspiration from the 80s for another eye-popping music video look in 2020.

The singer teamed up with The Black Eyed Peas for A Girl Like Me, which saw Shakira parade her endless legs, tiny waist and slender figure in a pair of black knickers, red crop top and sexy suspenders.

Shaking her hips and long blonde waves, the singer finished the vintage outfit with a pop of pink lipgloss and smoky eyes in another iconic music video look.

Steamy stage show

Shakira is one of the world's chart-topping artists, so it is no surprise that she has delighted fans at events such as the Billboard Music Awards.

In 2018, the singer dazzled as she took to the stage in a sheer black gown complete with thigh high splits, dripping in gold jewels that flowed down her gorgeous curves.

Shakira added an extra naughty twist with thigh high black boots, as she stared raunchily into the audience for the steamy performance.

Superbowl sensation

Pop icons Shakira and Jennifer Lopez were a match made in Super Bowl heaven when they joined forces for the half time show in 2020.

The singers dazzled in a number of sultry outfits, including silver and gold bodysuits for their performance of Waka, Waka.

Shakira later showed off her sizzling dance moves in a red tassel two-piece outfit, which flaunted her toned abs and sexy curves.

Red carpet icon

Shakira not only knows how to rock a stunning outfit onstage, but she knows how to light up the red carpet too.

She was the very essence of glamour at the FIFA Ballon d'Or Gala 2011, when the singer posed for photographs in a floorlength red gown, decorated with intricate details that framed the sweetheart bust.

The braless look was complimented by a red clutch bag, and styled with a retro inspired bob that screamed blond bombshell - and we loved it.
