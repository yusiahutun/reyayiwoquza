Dorcas Rigathi Offers Free Medical Camp at Hometown to Celebrate Birthday: &quot;Love and Compassion&quot;

  • Second Lady pastor Dorcas Rigathi was over the moon after turning a year older on Tuesday, October 17
  • She shared a sweet message as she acknowledged the goodness of God in her life and the honour of serving Kenyans
  • To celebrate her birthday, the woman of God disclosed she would sponsor a free medical camp in Gatanga constituency

Pastor Dorcas Rigathi is celebrating her birthday, and the woman of God wants to make it memorable.

Dorcas Rigathi's heart of gratitude

The wife of Deputy President Rigathi Gachagua took to social media to celebrate her special day with a lovely post.

As she turned a year older, she acknowledged God's grace and goodness that carried her through her life.

"As I celebrate another year of life, my heart is full of gratitude for the experiences the Lord has granted me. On this special day, I wish to thank God and Kenyans for the opportunity to serve," she wrote.

Where was pastor Dorcas born?

Dorcas also disclosed she wanted to give birth to the community where she spent most of her childhood.

The servant of God noted she would want to sponsor a free medical camp in her hometown as a show of love and an act of kindness.

"Again, because God saw it fit for the community of Ndunyu ya Chege, Gatanga Constituency, to be my place of birth and the home where I spent my childhood years. It is my joy to celebrate this birthday by offering a free medical camp as a gesture of love and compassion for the place and people that hold my dearest memories," she happily penned.
