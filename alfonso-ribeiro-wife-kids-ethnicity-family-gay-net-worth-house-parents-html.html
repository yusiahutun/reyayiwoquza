Alfonso Ribeiro, the host of ABC's America's Funniest Home Videos and a TV actor, is one lucky man to have such a wonderful family and married life.

Family Life Of Alfonso Ribeiro; Wife And Kids

Alfonso Ribeiro is married to the lovely Angela Unkrich and has a great family. He has been happily wedded to her since their wedding in 2012. Although they have a great friendship, this isn't Alfonso's first rodeo.

In the year 2002, he married Robin Stapler. The two originally met in 1999 while filming the television show "In the House," and were instantly smitten.

They did not, however, stand the test of time. Alfonso and Robin decided to call it quits after four years of marriage when they divorced in 2006. Sienna, their daughter, was shared between them.

Alfonso Ribeiro's daughter Ava Sue on 15 May 2019 (Photo: Alfonso Ribeiro's Instagram)

Ava is the newest member of their family. Her birthday is May 13th, 2019.

More On Alfonso Ribeiro:

Alfonso Ribeiro, an American actor best known for his performance in The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air starring Will Smith, was born on September 21, 1971, in The Bronx, New York. He went on to anchor the popular GSN game program Catch 21 as well as the controversial ABC show America's Funniest Home Videos.

His dancing abilities are also well-known, as he and his partner, Witney Carson, won the 19th season of Dancing With the Stars.

All of his accomplishments have garnered him all of the fame and money he has ever desired. His net worth is believed to be $7 million. No wonder he bought a huge house in the first place.
