Keira steps out with her old flame Jamie

    Pride And Prejudice beauty Keira Knightley just can't seem to stay away from on-off love Jamie Dornan. The young couple, who announced their split a few months ago, were spotted out on a London shopping trip this week. At one point the pair even held hands, showing that their feelings for each other still run deep.

    But its not clear if he's the only contender to be the starlet's Mr Darcy as she's also spoken of the charms of gorgeous Caracas-born co-star Edgar Ramirez, who shared her love scenes in the movie Domino.

    "Being topless was fine," said Keira of the scenes. "In fact, to be able to do that in the middle of the desert was actually quite liberating. And to do it with a beautiful Venezuelan - well, let's just say I'm a lucky girl!"

    Meanwhile the actress, currently working on Pirates Of The Caribbean II alongside Johnny Depp, is disappointed she doesn't get the chance to lock lips with the hunk. "In five years' time I'll be like, 'I'm sorry, I need to be guaranteed a snog with my leading man. Write it into my contract!'", said the Tinseltown favourite, who also revealed that her other Pirates co-star Orlando Bloom is a "marvellous kisser".
