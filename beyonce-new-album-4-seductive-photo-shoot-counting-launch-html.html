Beyonce new album 4: Seductive photo shoot counting down to launch

She's back and bringing it! Beyonce unveils stunning pictures from seductive photo shoot counting down to her new album 4

She is set to headline at Glastonbury this weekend but with the inclement weather predictions Beyonce might have to wear a little more than the outfits shown in her latest raunchy pictures.

Ahead of the launch of her new album 4, Beyonce,29, is unveiling a whole host of seductive photos which see the singer in a range of extremely provocative fans which will doubt please her fans before they even hear her music.

More photos will be revealed in the countdown to the launch on June 26.

Skills: Beyonce is unveiling a series of seductive photographs to her website which sees her take on a number of provocative poses in the countdown to her latest album launch

Strike a pose: Beyonce's hiatus from the music scene will be ended with her new album 4 released June 26

In two of the photos Beyonce is topless save for two furry gillets and her long blonde hair, while in another she is seen staring into a mirror in a corset.

She tries her circus skills out for size and balances with her legs spread positioned in between two ropes, while in another the Single Ladies singer shows off her couture pose in a white layered dress with a navy basque.

Speaking of her long awaited album her website declares: ‘4 is a twelve song collection born of her diverse sounds and sights Beyonce experience during her year-log time away from the spotlight. It is her self-described musical gumbo. Much like the dish, the album serves as a melting pot of musical styles punctuated by her undeniable vocal abilities.’

King B: Beyonce gets creative in the mirror

Beyonce's last album, her third solo album entitled I am... Sacha Fierce was released in 2008, and the in January 2010 she spoke about wanting to take a break from the music scene.

Speaking to USA Today Beyonce said: 'It's definitely time to take a break, to recharge my batteries. ... I'd like to take about six months and not go into the studio. I need to just live life, to be inspired by things again.'

Looking good: After a three year hiatus from music Beyonce is set to release her fourth solo album entitled 4

Couture creation: The singer, who is set to headline at the Glastonbury this weekend looks stunning in the new photos

Her latest album sees rapping from Kayne West on her Party track and I was Here was produced by former X Factor contestant Leona Lewis.

Discussing 4 Beyonce said: 'The album is definitely an evolution. It’s bolder than the music in my previous albums because I’m bolder. The more mature I become and the more life experiences I have, the more I have to talk about.'

She added: 'I really focused on songs being classics, songs that would last, songs that I could sing when I’m 40 and when I’m 60.”

On Sunday Beyonce will take to the Pyramid stage at Glastonbury to finish the four day festival with offerings from 4.
