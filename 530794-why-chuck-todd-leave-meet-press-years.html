Who is Chuck Todd?

He is an American TV journalist who was the 12th moderator of NBC's Meet the Press. Chuck graduated from Miami Killian Senior High School in Kendall, Miami. Afterwards, he attended George Washington University from 1990 to 1994. He declared a major in political science and a minor in music but did not earn a degree.

Before becoming a well-known TV and political analyst, Chuck ran political campaigns in Florida and Washington, DC. In 1992, he worked for Senator Tom Harkin's presidential campaign and worked part-time at the National Journal’s The Hotline from 1992 to March 2007.

He joined NBC in 2007. Todd became a political director for NBC News, providing on-air political analysis. He has worked in various roles within the media industry. He served as NBC's Chief White House correspondent and hosted The Daily Rundown on MSNBC.
