Rylee's father, Jimmy Butler III, was born on 14 September 1989 in Houston, Texas, United States. He had a troubled childhood as his father abandoned them in infancy. At 13, while living in the Houston suburb of Tomball, his mother kicked him out of the house.

He went to Tomball High School in Tomball, Texas. In high school, he averaged 10 points per game, and as a senior and team captain between 2006 and 2007, he averaged 19.9 points and 8.7 rebounds per game. He was voted the team's most valuable player.

Unfortunately, Jimmy was not heavily recruited when he finished high school, so he opted to join Tyler Junior College in Tyler, Texas. During his first year in college, he averaged 18.1 points, 7.7 rebounds, and 3.1 assists per game.

Later, he accepted an athletic scholarship to attend Marquette University as a Marquette Golden Eagles men's basketball player. As a sophomore during the 2008/2009 season, he averaged 5.6 points, 3.9 rebounds per game, and a 76.8% free throw percentage.

During the 2009/2010 season, he averaged 14.7 points and 6.4 rebounds per game. Subsequently, he earned the All-Big East Honorable Mention honours. In his senior year, he averaged 15.7 points per game during the 2010/2011 season.
