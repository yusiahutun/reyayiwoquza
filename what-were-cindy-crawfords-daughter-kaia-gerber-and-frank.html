What were Cindy Crawfords daughter Kaia Gerber and Frank Ocean up to at Loewes new NYC store?

Happenings: Spanish luxury fashion brand Loewe launched its first stand-alone store in New York with a star-studded party. The event began with cocktails at the new SoHo space – which, in addition to the brand’s runway collections and accessories, also features pieces from its growing art collection – followed by a holiday party at the Sinclair-Fletcher Mansion on the Upper East Side.

The A-list line-up of guests at the event included actors Maggie Gyllenhaal, Tessa Thompson, Jodie Comer and Chloë Sevigny, musician Frank Ocean, author Zadie Smith, model Soo Joo Park and soccer player Megan Rapinoe, while music was provided by Adam Bainbridge, Robyn and Kiddy Smile.

STYLE Edit: Loewe collection inspired by Scottish architecture

The new store has been designed to complement SoHo’s historic architecture, and the building it occupies underwent restoration with an emphasis on natural materials. Among the artworks on display in the store are a hand-painted screen by South African artist Lisa Brice; Hill, a woodcut by German artist Andrea Büttner; and a collection of vintage photographs by the late Sri Lankan photographer Lionel Wendt.
