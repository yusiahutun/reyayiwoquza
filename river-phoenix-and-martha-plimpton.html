About

Martha Plimpton is a 53 year old American Actress. Born Martha Campbell Plimpton on 16th November, 1970 in New York City, New York, USA, she is famous for her various roles in film, television, and stage, such as Jonsy in the 1984 independent family drama film The River Rat, Stephanie "Stef" Steinbrenner in the 1985 adventure comedy film The Goonies, Julie Buckman in the 1989 family comedy-drama film Parenthood, Samantha in the 1991 romantic comedy film Samantha, Jan in the 1996 romantic comedy-drama film Beautiful Girls, Patti Nyholm in the CBS legal drama series The Good Wife, Virginia Slims Chance in the Fox sitcom Raising Hope, Claire Rinato in the NBC police drama series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Eileen O’Neal in the ABC sitcom The Real O’Neals, and Megan in the HBO Max comedy-drama series Generation in a career that spans 1981–present. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.

American Actor River Phoenix was born River Jude Bottom on 23rd August, 1970 in Metolius, Oregon, United States and passed away on 31st Oct 1993 The Viper Room Nightclub in West Hollywood, Los Angeles, California, United States aged 23. He is most remembered for Stand By Me (1986). His zodiac sign is Leo.
