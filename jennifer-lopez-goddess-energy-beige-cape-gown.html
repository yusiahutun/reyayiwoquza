Jennifer Lopez's Gown Calls Back to Her Dreamy &quot;Maid in Manhattan&quot; Dress

She may always be Jenny From the Block, but sometimes Jennifer Lopez does switch out her Bronx-inspired street style for a dose of red-carpet glamour.

In her latest Instagram post, the “Can’t Get Enough” singer showed off a gorgeous beige Monique Lhuillier gown, which she wore for the Hispanic Federation’s 2024 annual gala. As she posed for the photos, the Golden Globe winner was giving serious goddess vibes in an elegant black-tie look constructed by her frequent stylists, Mariel Haenn and Rob Zangardi.

Her draped dress included gathered material all across the heart-shaped bodice and the wraparound skirt, which featured a high slit going up one of the legs. As a matching accessory, the neutral-toned ensemble included a floor-brushing cape that was pinned together at the front.

For accessories, J.Lo donned a diamond torque necklace from Messika that featured teardrop-shaped stones pointing towards the singer’s neck, like teeth. Mirroring the necklace, Lopez sported dangly light pink diamond earrings, which featured pointy silver encasings that wrapped partly around the stones, adding another sharp touch to the overall look. In one photo, a large chunky ring was also visible on the star’s finger.

Beauty-wise, the Hustlers star was giving Old Hollywood glitz with a blown-out wavy hairstyle that was parted on the side, courtesy of hairstylist Danielle Priano. Meanwhile, to match her blush-hued earrings, Lopez’s makeup’s artist, Scott Barnes, gave her a rosy lip and light pink eye shadow, and she also sported powdery pink nails from Elle Gerstein.

Fans who have been following J.Lo throughout her career might see some similarities between this look and the gown she wears during the memorable kissing scene in her 2002 romantic comedy Maid in Manhattan. While the dress in the movie lacked a cape, it featured a similar silhouette, and the nude-pink color wasn’t too far off from the hue of this Lhuillier design. Even Lopez’s necklaces share similarities.
