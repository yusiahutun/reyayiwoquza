Kirsten Dunst dons leather shorts as she's supported by Jesse Plemons at her Observed By Us event

Kirsten Dunst looked lovely as she flaunted her long legs in a pair of black leather shorts at the Coach x Observed By Us launch event in Los Angeles on Wednesday.

The 41-year-old Bring It On actress - who attended the Cannes Film Festival earlier this year - teamed the bottoms with a white eyelet blouse. 

Dunst was supported by her husband, actor Jesse Plemons, as he attended the event in an Observed By Us embellished denim jacket.

She rounded out the classic look with a pair of black Mary Jane heels.

Dunst wore her shoulder-grazing blonde locks in a side part with the ends slightly flipped and the front pieces tucked behind her ears.

Kirsten was joined at the event by her Observed By Us co-creator, illustrator Jessica Herschko.

Supportive husband: Dunst was supported by her husband, actor Jesse Plemons, as he attended the event in an Observed By Us embellished denim jacket

Kirsten, who hails from New Jersey, added a pop of color to her look with a bright shade of coral lipstick.

Her delicate and feminine long-sleeved button-up shirt also had understated embroidered color accents along its collar.

The Marie Antoinette star coordinated her ensemble with a black leather purse from the newly launched fashion line.

It was embellished with illustrations of a palm tree, a red heart, and a lit candle stick. 

Jessica, who designed Observed By Us with Kirsten, dressed in an embroidered black blazer for the occasion.

Her self-designed look was covered in imagery like gold stars, a spaceship, and a moon. 

Herschko wore the jacket over a graphic T-shirt and rocked black bottoms.

She incorporated bits of color with aqua blue eyeliner along her bottom lash line and a matte pink shade of lipstick. 

Business partners: Kirsten was joined at the event by her Observed By Us co-creator, illustrator Jessica Hershko

Fashionable: The 41-year-old Bring It On actress teamed her shorts with a white eyelet blouse

Kirsten first announced her fashion line in an Instagram post on June 27th.

Sharing a photo of actress and The Bear star Ayo Edebiri wearing a blouse from the range, she alerted her 2.2 million followers.

'So excited that @ayoedebiri wore our @observedbyus x @coach collection! Coming soon to all of you!!!!!!'

Edebiri wrote in the comments, 'SO FUN AND COOL AND GOOD UR THE GREATEST,' and the post has racked up over 16,000 likes.

Coach also took to social media to spread the work about the collaboration with its 6.8 million followers.

In a joint post with creative director Stuart Vevers, the caption read, 'Introducing Coach x Observed By Us.

'A special collab featuring vibrant color and hand-drawn illustration, created in partnership with Jessica Herschko and Kirsten Dunst.

'Let your imagination run wild and shop at the link in bio.' 

L-R: Jessica, Stuart Vevers, and Kirsten posed for a photo at the event

Setup: Guests were treated to an outdoor meal at a long, beautifully set poolside table

On Wednesday, Kirsten proudly shared a snapshot of a WWD article promoting the new launch.

She wrote in the caption, 'LAUNCH DAY!!! Observed By Us X COACH. So excited to share this news!!!'

Hilary Duff also attended the swanky event, which was held poolside on a rooftop in LA.

Sharing photos of the actress and husband Matthew Koma on Instagram, Observed By Us wrote in a post on Thursday, 'Thank you @hilaryduff and @matthewkoma for coming to celebrate our collaboration! Was so nice having you there!'
