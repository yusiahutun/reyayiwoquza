90,000 Passes Already Sold For Rock Am Ring &amp; Rock Im Park Festivals

NUREMBURG, Germany (VIP-NEWS) — Approximately 90,000 festival tickets have been successfully sold for Rock am Ring and Rock im Park, marking a robust demand and heightened anticipation from fans.

As the organizers, DreamHaus and ARGO Konzerte, assess the situation almost four months ahead of the 2024 festival weekend, they reveal that tickets are going fast, particularly in premium camping categories.

The festivals are scheduled to take place from June 7th to 9th, 2024, at the Nürburgring and in Nuremberg.

In early November, Eventimpresents, DreamHaus, and Argo Konzerte unveiled the complete program for all stages, featuring confirmed performances by renowned artists such as Green Day, Die Ärzte, Måneskin, Avenged Sevenfold, Billy Talent, Broilers, Kraftklub, and Queens Of The Stone Age.

Comparatively, the 2023 edition of the festivals drew approximately 80,000 attendees to Rock am Ring and 70,000 to Rock im Park, slightly fewer than the 2022 figures of 90,000 and 75,000, respectively. The cumulative attendance for both festivals in 2022 was 165,000.
