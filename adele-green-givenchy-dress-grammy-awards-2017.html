Adele Wears Green Givenchy Dress at the 2017 Grammy Awards Red Carpet

Hello, it's Adele. The singer just made an appearance on the Grammy Awards red carpet in an unexpected but nonetheless stunning gown by Givenchy Couture.

The army green dress is already a major win for the the songstress, boasting checkered detailing on the torso, sleeves dotted with beads, sheer lace details on the arms and a shimmery, pleated skirt embellished with lace panels. She finished off the look with a gold brooch and several rings on her fingers. (However, she's not wearing one on the ring finger on her left hand, despite spurring wedding rumors when she was seen sporting one recently.) Her perfectly swept updo makes the final touch.

Adele is nominated for five awards this evening including Record of the Year. As one of the first major celebs to make an entrance this evening, she's set the bar high for the rest of the nominees and A-listers attending.
