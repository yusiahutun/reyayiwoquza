Pornhub searches for Stormy Daniels skyrocket

Online searches for porn star Stormy Daniels (R) have skyrocketed since allegations emerged that the Trump campaign paid her off to keep quiet about a supposed affair

Online searches for porn star Stormy Daniels have skyrocketed over the past week after allegations emerged that the Donald Trump presidential campaign paid her hush money over an alleged sexual encounter.   

Pornhub Insights, a blog that details data analytics for porn streaming website Pornhub, says that worldwide searches for the 38-year-old starlet have risen an eye popping 375 per cent since January 14.  

Those numbers were recorded just two days after an explosive report by the Wall Street Journal claimed Trump paid her $130,000 to keep quiet about their alleged fling just before he entered the 2016 presidential election. 

'[Adult Video News] Hall of Fame porn star Stormy Daniels has appeared in adult videos since the early 2000s, but recently shot to the forefront of public attention when her back-door dealings with US President Trump were revealed,' the blog revealed. 

According to Newsweek, which requested the information from Pornhub, searches for Stormy Daniels averaged around 2,500 a week. But that number shot up to more than 2 million in just five days following the publication of the Journal article.

Newsweek noted that Washington DC had the highest concentration of searches in the continental United States, peaking at 332 per cent above the two-week average on Jan. 14 - compared to 272 per cent throughout the rest of the country.

Daniels, whose real name is Stephanie Clifford, took and passed a polygraph test in 2011 as she talked about her 'textbook generic' sexual relations with a married Donald Trump more than a decade ago, In Touch Weekly claims.

'We had really good banter. He told me once that I was someone to be reckoned with, beautiful and smart just like his daughter,' Daniels told the tabloid in a story released by the publication this week.

Her alleged affair with the future president began at the American Century celebrity golf tournament at the Edgewood Tahoe Golf Course in Stateline, Nevada, in July 2006, several months after Trump's youngest son Barron Trump was born to his wife Melania. 

Daniels described their initial sexual encounter with the billionaire businessman as 'nothing crazy' and admitted to the tabloid the two didn't use protection. 'It was one position, what you would expect someone his age to do,' Daniels said. 

The Wall Street Journal story claims that Trump's Lawyer, Michael Cohen, made the hefty payment to Daniels just before the presidential election season.   

Cohen has denied that Daniels and Trump had a sexual encounter, but has not specifically denied giving the porn star payment.

Daniels hasn't said anything publicly except to deny the relationship.
