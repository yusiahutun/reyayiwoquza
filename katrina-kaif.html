Katrina Kaif is a British actress and model who primarily works in the Indian film industry, popularly known as Bollywood. She was born on July 16, 1983, in Hong Kong, to a British mother and an Indian father. Katrina spent her childhood in various countries due to her mother's charity work. She started modeling at the age of 14 and later moved to Mumbai, India, to pursue a career in acting. Katrina made her Bollywood debut in 2003 with the film Boom, but it was her role in the 2005 film Sarkar that brought her recognition. She has since acted in several successful Bollywood films, including Namastey London, Singh Is Kinng, Ek Tha Tiger, Jab Tak Hai Jaan, and Tiger Zinda Hai, among others. Apart from acting, Katrina is also known for her dance performances and has been a part of several item numbers. She has endorsed several leading brands and is considered one of the most popular and attractive actresses in India.
