Are Dump Trucks Responsible For Broken Windshields?

published on June 01, 2024
Dump trucks can cause broken windshields, often by debris falling from their cargo. Secure covering on these trucks can prevent such incidents.  Having a broken windshield can be both dangerous and frustrating, especially if you suspect a dump truck’s cargo is to blame. Objects escaping from the open beds of these vehicles pose a hazard on roadways, often leading to chips and cracks in the glass of trailing cars.  Dump truck operators are mandated by law to ensure their loads are adequately covered to prevent debris from escaping.
