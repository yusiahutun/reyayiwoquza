YouTube Partners With Ticketmaster, See Tickets And Eventbrite

published on February 24, 2024
LONDON (CelebrityAccess) – YouTube has announced partnerships with Ticketmaster, See Tickets and Eventbrite in the UK and Ireland. As of this week, fans watching videos on Official Artists Channels will see ticket listings for live music performances and will have the option to click a ‘Tickets’ button to purchase tickets directly from one of the ticketing partners. The new feature is reportedly an expansion of a ticketing integration first launched with Ticketmaster in the US, which has since been expanded with Eventbrite and rolled out across Canada, Australia and New Zealand.
