Behind the label: Colombo Via Della Spiga

published on June 23, 2024
Who started it? After inheriting her family’s leather company, Italian designer Luisa Colombo opened her first store in Milan in 1953. Beginning with saddles and harnesses, she expanded into handbags in 1955. The handbags quickly became known for their craftsmanship and individuality, and were made of exotic leathers such as crocodile, lizard and snake skin. Today, the brand is known for its heritage and commitment to quality. It has boutiques in Milan, Seoul and, as of June, Hong Kong. Read More...
