2024 Grammy Awards Winners: See the List

The winners at the 2024 Grammy Awards have been announced, and the list includes some of the biggest names in country music today.

Chris Stapleton  and Lainey Wilson were the biggest winners in country music. Stapleton was first up, taking home Best Country Solo Performance at the 2024 Grammys, and just moments later, he also won Best Country Song for the same song, "White Horse."

Wilson gave an impassioned speech when she took home Best Country Album for Bell Bottom Country, likening winning her first Grammy during her first-ever Grammy Awards ceremony to a "harvest of a lifetime" after carefully growing the work.

Most of the country categories were announced during the non-televised portion of the Grammys show on Sunday afternoon, during which many of the winners in Americana, Latin, classical, jazz and other less commercial categories were also handed out, along with a number of awards in technical categories.

Brandy Clark and Jason Isbell were also among country music's big winners in 2024. See a full list of country winners below.

RELATED: See the All-Genre Winners at the 2024 Grammy Awards

The Grammys were particularly country-centric in 2024, with Jelly Roll and the War and Treaty both landing nominations in the all-genre Best New Artist category.

Willie Nelson, Brothers Osborne, Morgan Wallen and Carly Pearce were also among the country nominees, while Stapleton, Zach Bryan, Tyler Childers and Brandy Clark led the country / folk / Americana nominees with three apiece.

The 2024 Grammy Awards aired live from Los Angeles on Feb. 4 via CBS and Paramount+.
