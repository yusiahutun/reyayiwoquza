Though we all have the same skin structure, yet every skin is different. When we talk about dark skin, it's characterized by high levels of melanin that needs special care. Melanin-producing cells (melanocytes) may be more prone to injury and inflammation (1). Hence, investing in a suitable moisturizer is indeed an important step in your skincare routine. Here we've mentioned some of the best moisturizers for black skin that promise the utmost radiance and glow and hydrate your skin. 

Unlike moisturizers for lighter skin tones, lotion for dark skin have extra-emollient properties and skin-loving contents to protect the skin from damage. Further, they help manage melanin levels and associated issues like hyperpigmentation. Since black skin is more prone to irritation, pimples, dryness, and other dermal problems, finding the right moisturizer becomes a necessity. The below-featured products are popular among beauty fanatics with black skin to get a natural glow and enjoy it all year round.
