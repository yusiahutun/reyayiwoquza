Brian Chiras Grandfather Calls out People Who Leaked Grandsons Raw Images: Be Ethical

  • Brian Chira died in March and was laid to rest at his grandmother’s birthplace in Githunguri, Kiambu county
  • Chira’s grandfather talked about his late grandson, noting that he was an obedient boy who followed instructions
  • He said that their family did not know that the late 23-year-old was deep into alcoholism, as he never drank while at home
  • Chira's grandfather said that the late TikToker never said he was gay, and he only saw the claims online after his death

Late TikTok star Brian Chira’s grandfather has spoken after his demise.

During an interview with presenter Ali, Chira’s grandfather spoke of his relationship with the late TikTok star.

He described the late 23-year-old as a soft-spoken young man who was very obedient and always did as was told.

"He wowed many with his actions. When sent to the shop, he would hurriedly return home. He was very obedient. When his mum would come here, he would help her. His mum was a computer studies teacher in some high school here."

Was Brian Chira gay?

The grandfather denied claims that Chira was gay, claiming that he saw the allegations online and that his grandson never talked about his orientation.

"We knew him as a silent person. He was soft-spoken you would want him to keep talking. He was not loud and we knew him as a good boy. We were shocked that he had a drinking problem. He was a bright boy. It's something which I've never heard, even through media, even from Brian. I never heard from him even one time. What I heard from his is 'I want to be a reader, economist, I want to start my broadcasting centre' but such I never heard from him."

He condemned netizens who shared the late TikToker’s photos on social media, asking them to maintain ethics.

How Brian Chira died

An eyewitness who helped Chira before he met his untimely death revealed he found him outside an entertainment joint in Kiambu.

He said he arrived at the establishment around 2:53 am on March 16. Chira asked him for help. He offered him a lift, and together, they took a boda boda home.

He alleged that after arriving at his destination, Chira alighted, and while crossing the road, he was hit by a speeding vehicle.
