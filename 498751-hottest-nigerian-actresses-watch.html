15 hottest Nigerian actresses that you should watch in 2024

The Nigerian movie industry is home to some of the best talent in Africa. Also known as Nollywood, the industry has produced some of the best content in drama, comedy and romance. If you are a fan of local movies, then you might be wondering who the top and upcoming stars are in 2024. so who are they? And who are the hottest Nigerian actresses you should watch out for?

Nollywood ranks top among film industries with the hottest female actresses. These stars have featured in various well-known flicks cementing their names as favourites among fans. However, in recent times, new characters have emerged in the industry catching the attention of fans. These female stars have the beauty, talent, and brains to make it big in the industry. So, who are the hottest women in Nollywood?

Hottest Nigerian actresses to watch in 2024

From Mercy Johnson to Ini Edo, the hottest Nigerian actresses in Nollywood includes some of the best names. These stars have featured in some of the highest-grossing films in Nollywood and are the most sought-after in the local film industry. Below is a list of the 15 celebrities you should watch out for in 2024.

1. Adesua Etomi

Tolulope Adesua Etomi-Wellington, also known as Adesua Etomi, is a Nigerian star considered by fans among the most beautiful actresses in Nigeria. She is 36 years old in 2024 and was born in Owerri, Imo State, on 22 February 1988.

Adesua's filmography includes flicks like The Wedding Party, The Arbitration and Falling. She has secured various accolades and awards, including the 2016 Africa Magic Viewers Choice Award for Best Actress in a Drama.

2. Adunni Ade

Adunni Ade is a Nigerian actress and fashion model who has appeared in films such as Jenifa's Diary, The New Normal, and The Silent Baron. She is well-known for her beauty as well as her professionalism. She was born on 7 June 1976, in Queens, New York, to a German American mother and a Lagos-born Nigerian father.

3. Beverly Naya

Beverly Naya is a well-known female actress. She was born in London, England, on 17 April 1989 and is 34 years old in 2024. She is the only child in her family, and her parents are from Nigeria.

Naya began acting at age 17 while studying scriptwriting and film-making at Roehampton University. She has appeared in various flicks, including Guilty Pleasures, Death Waters and Chief Daddy 2: Going for Broke.

4. Bimbo Ademoye

Bimbo Ademoye is a Nigerian actress best known for her comedic films and TV roles. She has appeared in films such as Sugar Rush, Anikulapo, and Breaded Life.

Bimbo Ademoye was born on 4 February 1991 in Lagos, Nigeria. She received a nomination for Best Actress in a Comedy Series at the 2020 AMVCAs. In addition, Ademoye won Best Actress in a Supporting Role at the 2020 Best Of Nollywood Awards.

5. Bisola Aiyeola

Bisola Aiyeola is an actress and singer from Nigeria. She is known for being the first runner-up of Big Brother Naija in 2017. In 2018, she won the AMVCA Trailblazer Award, and in 2021, she became one of the investors of Amaze, an investment app.

Bisola Aiyeola was born on 21 January 1986 in Nigeria and is 38 years old in 2024. She holds a business management degree from The National Open University of Nigeria.

6. Chacha Eke Faani

Charity Eke, also known as Chacha Eke Faani, is a famous actress from Ebonyi State in Nigeria. She has featured in many films and TV shows in her career, with her breakout role in 2012 in the drama flick, The End is Near.

Chacha holds an accounting degree from Eboyi State University. She was born on 17 July 1987 in Ebonyi State, Nigeria and is 36 years old in 2024.

7. Damilola Adegbite

Oluwadamilola Adegbite, also known as Damilola Adegbite, is an actress, model, and TV personality from West Africa. She is famous for featuring in Tinsel, a soap opera as Thelema Duke. Damilola was born on 18 May 1985 in Surulere, Lagos State, Nigeria. Apart from her film career, the star has been featured in various TV commercials and as a host.

8. Genevieve Nnaji

Genevieve Nnaji is among the top names when mentioning beautiful Nigerian actresses. She has been in the industry for 36 years and counting. Genevieve received various accolades, including Best Actress in a Leading Role in 2005 and the Order of the Federal Republic for her contributions to Nollywood.

Genevieve Nnaji was born on 3 May 1979 in Mbaise, Imo State, Nigeria. She is 44 years old in 2024, and her zodiac sign is Taurus.

9. Ini Edo

Iniobong Edo Ekim is a well-known Nigerian model and actress. She began her acting career in 2000 and had over 100 credits. Ini Edo, born on 23 April 1982, will be 42 years old in April 2024. The actress was named a United Nations Habitat Youth Envoy in 2014. Some of Edo's works include Shanty Town (2023), Gbege (2022) and Nkoyo (2021).

10. Jemima Osunde

Jemima Osunde is among the top young Nigerian actresses to watch in 2024. She is also a model and presenter. She was born on 30 April 1996 and is 27 years old in 2024.

Jemima's breakout role came in 2018 when she played Leila in the popular TV series Shuga. The same year, the star received a nomination for Best Actress in a Leading Role at the 15th Africa Movie Academy Awards for her role in The Delivery Boy.

11. Lilian Esoro

Lilian Esoro debuted on-screen in 2005, starring in the soap opera Extended Family. Since then, she has appeared in productions including The Potter, Secret Room (2013) and Couple of Days (2016). In addition, Esoro received a nomination for Best Actress in A Comedy Series and Best African Actress in 2018.

12. Mercy Aigbe

Mercy Aigbe is an actress, director, fashionista and businesswoman from West Africa. She began her acting career in 2001, featuring in low-budget productions. Mercy's big screen debut came in 2006 when she featured in Ara.

Mercy Aigbe was born on 1 January 1978 and is 46 years old in 2024. She is an entrepreneur and founder of Mercy Aigbe Gentry School of Drama.

13. Nancy Isime

Nancy Isime is a young Nigerian actress well known for her roles in Blood Sisters (2022), Obara'm (2022) and Shanty Town (2023). She began her career as an actress in Echoes in 2011. In addition to films, Nancy is a television presenter featuring in shows, What's Hot, MTN Project Fame season 7 and The Squeeze.

14. Osas Ighodaro

Osariemen Martha Elizabeth Ighodaro, also known as Osas Ighodaro, is a Nigerian American actress, producer, host and humanitarian. She founded the Joyful Joy Foundation, an initiative for fighting malaria. Osas is also the 2010 Miss Black USA Pageant and a presenter/host. She featured in MTV's Shuga in 2019 and as Adanna (Danni) in the 2012 soap Tinsel.

15. Regina Daniels

Regina Daniels is your girl if you are looking for young Nigerian actresses. The actress started her career aged seven. Regina Daniels was born on 10 October 2000 to a famous Nollywood actress, Rita Daniels. She is 23 years old in 2024, and her zodiac sign is Libra. Regina's first movie was Marriage of Sorrow, followed by Miracle Child in 2010.
