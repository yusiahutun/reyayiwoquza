Playa Bowls to Open in Stuart

Playa Bowls will now call Stuart home. The brand will unveil its newest location (1925 NW Federal Highway) with a kick-off celebration May 20, featuring free bowls and limited-edition t-shirts for the first 50 guests in line, along with other giveaways and entertainment provided throughout the day. 

The Stuart shop is owned and operated by franchise owners Michael and Jenna Zorn. With plans to open a second Playa Bowls location later this summer in Jupiter, the husband-and-wife duo saw an opportunity to grow their passion for the nutritious bowl concept and expand Playa Bowlsâ€™ footprint in Florida.  

The menu boasts curated superfood creations, but guests are also encouraged to customize their own. Each bowl begins with a superfood base, including acai, pitaya, mango, chia pudding, coconut, kale, banana, oatmeal, and more. Next come tasty and nutritious toppings, such as fresh cut fruits, seeds, nuts, granola, dried berries, and nut butters. The super fruit bowl shop also offers a selection of juices, smoothies, cold brew, and other select drinks. Playa Bowls can be made vegan or gluten-free, and can be adapted for Whole30, Keto, and Paleo diets.  

The Stuart shop will be open daily, from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m.
