ACM Awards 2023 red carpet: See all the jaw-dropping looks from Miranda Lambert, Carly Pearce, &amp; mor

The best-dressed at the 58th Academy of Country Music Awards

    Country music's wildest party, the 2023 ACM Awards, landed in Texas on Thursday and the stars did not disappoint on the black carpet.

    Despite a major last-minute change after one act was forced to cancel their performance, country music's biggest stars donned their most show-stopping outfits for a night to remember. The likes of Miranda Lambert, Lainey Wilson, Carly Pearce, Ashley McBryde and so many more graced the Ford Center at The Star in Frisco in jaw-dropping looks – think figure-hugging dresses, bold colors, sequins, and plenty of fringe detailing. 

    And as if showcasing their flamboyant fashion wasn't enough, many also took to the stage for a memorable performance, with the incredible line-up including Jason Aldean, Kane Brown, Luke Combs, Cody Johnson, Dolly Parton, Jelly Roll, Keith Urban, and Ed Sheeran, who made his ACM Awards debut. 

    Check out the best-dressed stars as they arrived below…

    Miranda Lambert

    Miranda Lambert looked like a total bombshell in her plunging royal blue dress that featured sequin cups, a provocative thigh-split, and a subtle train.

    Lainey Wilson

    Lainey Wilson stuck to her signature bell bottoms but added a twist with silk fabric, a cut-out wrap top with floor-length sleeves, and of course, her trademark hat.

    Gabby Barrett 

    Gabby Barrett brought some dramatic flair to the carpet and looked exquisite in this rich black velvet gown that boasted a figure-hugging fit and wonderfully elaborate sleeves.

    Carly Pearce

    Carly Pearce looked beautiful in her elegant strapless dress with ruched detailing at the waist and a leg-baring split.

     Nicole Kidman

    Nicole Kidman was on hand to support her husband, Keith Urban, and she looked super chic in her tweed suit with flared pants, adding a little sass with a plunging neckline.

     Dolly Parton

    Dolly Parton, who finally revealed whether she is covered in tattoos, went all out in this gunmetal ensemble that featured sequins, a plunging neckline, and fringe detailing along the top and pants.

    Caylee Hammack 

    Singer-songwriter Caylee Hammack looked sensational in her romantic ruffled pink dress which contrasted beautifully against her auburn hair.

    Ashely McBryde

     Ashley McBryde got the memo about sequins and rocked this subtle black frock with glittering embellishment that also featured a front split.

    Priscilla Block

    Priscilla Block looked stunning in her figure-hugging black dress with a sheer overlay, high collar, and silver detailing.

    Tenille Townes

    Tenille Townes opted for this gorgeous blue suit with delicate studded detailing and a white silky crop top.

    MacKenzie Porter

    MacKenzie Porter stood out in her blue sequinned two-piece with a matching cape that boasted structured shoulders and a subtle train.

    Pillbox Patti

    Pillbox Patti turned heads in her dazzling frock, and it's not just because of the curve-hugging silhouette or the bright pink sequins. The dramatic sleeve featured a built-in shelf that she used to place miniature bottles of alcohol!

    Ashley Cooke

    Ashley Cooke went bold for color in this stunning metallic teal strapless gown.

    Tiera Kennedy

    Tiera Kennedy also rocked a sequinned suit but in a cool silver hue. 

    Cheyenne Kimball

    Cheyenne Kimball glittered in gold with hints of green sequins and a front slit.
