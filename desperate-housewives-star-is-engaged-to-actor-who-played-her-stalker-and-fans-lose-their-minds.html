Desperate Housewives star is engaged to actor who played her stalker and fans lose their minds

Desperate Housewives fans got the shock of their lives when they found out two very unlikely characters from the show, Julie and Eddie are actually engaged in real life thanks to a viral TikTok.

The TV show, set on Wisteria Lane, aired from 2004 – 2012 for 180 episodes. Fans eagerly followed the lives of the group of women, seen through the eyes of their friend and neighbor who passed away during the pilot episodes. However, it looks like fans haven’t been watching the actual actors’ lives as eagerly, as they’ve only just found out that Andrea Bowen, who played Julie Mayer is actually engaged to her strangler on the show in real life.

Desperate Housewives characters Julie and Eddie are engaged in real life

Fans of the hit show will have the strangling storyline engraved in their heads, when it was revealed the killer that’s been stalking Wisteria Lane was, in fact, Eddie Orlofsky, played by Josh Zuckerman.

He also tried to kill Julie, thinking she was Susan. He attempted to strangle her to death, but when realized it was actually Mayer he was strangling, he ran away from the scene.

In the show, she survived the strangle, and in real life, she actually got engaged to the man who almost murdered her. Plot twist and a half.

Although their relationship “started in 2009, then again in 2018, then again in 2020,” some fans of the show are only just finding out now about it, as a TikTok made by a fan goes viral.

One fan even found the celebrity couple’s wedding website, which they posted to Reddit, which read: “It all started in 2009, then again in 2018, then again in 2020.

“We won’t go too in-depth here with our ‘how we met’ story but it’s your basic boy meets girl, boy tries to kill girl, 9 years later boy and girl get pregnant with twins, girl ghosts boy (according to boy..girl doesn’t agree with this assessment) and a few years later they go on a first date. Classic..”

Fortunately, this was all a joke, and everything that was mentioned happened fictionally and not in real life. However, their love is in fact well and truly real.

Fans can’t get over the unlikely pairing

The internet is slowly but surely starting to find out about the revelation, and everyone is just in shock, in a good way.

“Desperate Housewives is my fave and I never knew they were together!!!?? This has made my day!” exclaimed one.

“Omgggg I’m gonna cry,” penned another.

“Whaaaaaat my two fav characters,” a third excited fan wrote.

One joked: “I don’t think your mom will be happy to know you’re dating Eddie Orlofsky.”
