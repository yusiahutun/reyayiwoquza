Tom Girardi Split With Erika Jayne

Tom Girardi and his reality star wife, Erika Jayne, have called off their marriage. USA Today mentioned that Girardi's ex-wife, who appeared in Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, opened about their split via her representative on 3 November 2020. 'After much consideration, I have decided to end my marriage to Tom Girardi. This is not a step taken lightly or easily,' she said.

Similarly, she added that she has great love and respect for Tom and their years and the lives they built together.

Thomas Girardi and his recently separated wife, Erika Jayne (©: People)

Stating more about their separation, Erika Jayne asked to respect their privacy. She mentioned, 'It is my absolute wish to proceed through this process with respect and with the privacy that both Tom and I deserve. I request that others give us that privacy as well.'

At age 81, Tom Girardi had been enjoying marital bliss with the 49 years old reality star for twenty-one years since 1999. They were a pinnacle of a sustained romantic bond despite having a huge age gap of 48 years. They first met in Los Angeles while Jayne was working as a waitress and drifted a friendship. After a year-long friendship, the duo started dating, and their wedding came a few years later in 1999.

Tom Girardi's Past Relationships

Erika Jayne is not the first wife of an American attorney Tom Girardi. He has been married three times, and Jayne is the latest woman with whom Girardi had an affair.

Tom Girardi was married to his first wife named Karen Weitzul Giraldi, on 29 August 1964. They shared their marital life for a few years and ended up dispatching their romantic life. Later in September 1993, Girardi shared wedding vows with his second wife, Kathy Riser, which also ended up in a divorce.

Tom Girardi, his ex-wife, Erika Jayne, and her son Thomas Zizzo Jr. (©: Superbhub)

Later, he married the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, Erika Jayne, in 1999. He is a step-dad to Jayne's child (son), Thomas Zizzo Jr., from her previous relationship.

Tom Girardi's Net Worth

Tom Girardi, an American Attorney, has a net worth of $30 million. He is also the first attorney to bag a $1 million award for medical malpractice cases in California.

Tom took the cases against the companies, including Lockheed Corp, Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority, and Pacific Gas & Electric Co. One of his famous cases, Pacific Gas & Electric Co., was brought to life with a movie named Erin Brokovich, which awarded $333 million to 650 residents in Hinkley, California.

Besides that, Girardi is also a founding partner of the Los Angeles, California law firm Girardi & Keese. Currently, Girardi resides in his 1920s-era house in Pasadena, California.
