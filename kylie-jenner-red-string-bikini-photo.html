Kylie Jenner Takes In the Sun in a Fiery Red String Bikini

Kylie Jenner is sun-kissed and unbothered, and flaunting yet another sultry bikini look this summer.

On Instagram yesterday, the Kylie Cosmetics founder shared her latest bikini look while sunbathing on a lounge chair outside what appears to be older sister Kendall Jenner’s home. In a photo posted yesterday as part of an Instagram carousel, Jenner flaunts her figure in a fiery red string triangle bikini—which seems to be from her supermodel sister’s FWRD—while lying out with her arms over her head in a sexy pose. In another photo, Jenner poses in an upright position while holding a glass of red wine.

For the lounge sesh, the beauty mogul kept her makeup minimal and accessorized with just a simple pair of gold huggie hoop earrings, maximizing her natural glow.

“Summer feeling,” she captioned the post.

Even when it’s not summer, the youngest Jenner regularly documents her bikini looks on Instagram. During a tropical getaway trip back in February, the mogul also shared photos of herself posing poolside in a shimmering lavender lamé bikini from Blue Lagoon Swimwear that featured a halter neckline with crisscross straps. Earlier that week, she flaunted a rhinestone-studded neon two-piece from Chanel, featuring a blue-and-pink color-blocked top paired with a lime-green bottom.

That same month, Jenner matched with older sister Kim Kardashian in sultry triangle black bikinis. In a photo posted on Jenner’s Instagram, the sisters face one another while kneeling on a lounge chair by the pool. “You my twin 🤞🏼,” Jenner captioned the pic. “Always and Forever,” Kardashian wrote back.

When she's not in a bikini, Jenner casually graces us with her street style.

On a recent trip to Paris, she debuted a string of elegant, monochromatic looks. These included two all-white white ensembles—a white halter midi dress from Alaïa and a two-piece miniskirt-blazer set from Maximilian Davis’s fall/winter 2022 collection. She also wowed in a chic black halter dress, courtesy of Bottega Veneta, while out in the romantic city.
