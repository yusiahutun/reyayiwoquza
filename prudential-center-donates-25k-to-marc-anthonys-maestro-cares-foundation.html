Prudential Center Donates $25K To Marc Anthonys Maestro Cares Foundation

Marc Anthony (Photo Credit: Tom Horak)

NEWARK, NJ (CelebrityAccess) – On Saturday (February 10), the electrifying Marc Anthony took center stage at Prudential Center in Newark, NJ, marking his tenth consecutive performance during Valentine’s Day weekend as part of his Historia Tour. The sold-out crowd experienced a night filled with dancing and laughter as Anthony once again showcased his unparalleled talent.

The journey began on February 14, 2014, when Anthony first graced the stage at the Prudential Center. Before Anthony’s most recent performance, Prudential Center executives Sean Saadeh (Executive Vice President, Entertainment) and Matt Saril (Director, Entertainment Marketing) presented Anthony with a generous check for $25,000 to his Maestro Cares Foundation. Since 2014, the venue has contributed over $400,000 to the Maestro Cares Foundation.

The Maestro Cares Foundation is dedicated to improving the quality of life for disadvantaged children and communities in Latin America and the United States. The foundation achieves this noble goal by providing essential resources, housing, and educational programming to support the growth and development of children, thus empowering them to become leaders in their communities.

The donation exemplifies Prudential Center’s dedication to community-focused initiatives that align with the social causes important to the acts and artists who perform there.
