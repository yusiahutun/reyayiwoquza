She was enjoying a quiet stroll as she ran errands in her local neighbourhood.

But Reese Witherspoon suffered a wardrobe malfunction at the hands of mother nature as she stepped out in Brentwood, California, last week.

The 37-year-old actress fell victim to a sudden gust of wind, which whipped up to reveal her pert derriere to everyone in the street.

Oops-a-daisy! Reese Witherspoon suffered a wardrobe malfunction at the hands of mother nature as she stepped out in Brentwood, California

Handling the somewhat embarrassing situation with grace and elegance, the star simply laughed off the mishap as she carried on her day.

Giggling away as she ambled down the street, the mother-of-three seemed in great spirits as she ran errands, in spite of her calamitous outfit.

Perhaps Reese's outfit choice of a flimsy red printed skirt and seemingly skimpy underwear wasn't the most practical given the day's windy conditions.

Whoops! The actress revealed more than she bargained for when her dress got caught by a gust of wind

The Oscar-winning actress teamed her skirt with a chic white blazer and matching vest, gold chain pendants and Ray Ban shades.

Towering heels and a pristine white leather handbag completed her low-key daytime look.

As was well publicised, Reese was arrested for disorderly conduct in Georgia, Atlanta, back in April after her husband Jim Toth was pulled over for driving under the influence.

The actress managed to get herself arrested after drunkenly ranting at the arresting officer and failing to stay inside the vehicle.

So funny: A laughing Reese was spotted out in Los Angeles on Tuesday and was wearing a very short skirt

It's hilarious! The actress looked bright and breezy in her summery  ensemble

A new woman: Witherspoon was on good form as she headed out to the shops, a day after returning from a trip to Paris

But almost two months on from the drama, Reese seems to be trying to put the incident behind her - happily smiling for photographers on her shopping trip on Tuesday while gamely performing her role as the loveable girl next door.

On Monday, Reese wore a similar beaming smile when she landed at Los Angeles International airport after a week in Paris.

She let her natural beauty shine as she strolled through the busy terminal, with no lipstick or other visible evidence of make-up.

Parisian style rubbing off? Witherspoon wore a particular bright, stylish outfit with a white blazer and top and a bright patterned orange skirt that showed off her shapely legs

Careful steps: Not only was the grass not right for her shoes, but a breeze blew through her skirt and almost created an embarrassing wardrobe malfunction

The This Means War star sported casual jeans and grey jumper over a white T-shirt with a grey-patterned scarf adding a fashionable flair.

She let her trademark blonde hair hang loose, and she wore it parted to the side and tucked behind her ears schoolgirl-style. 

Rugged tan cowgirl boots with low heels completed her tomboyish ensemble, while the Louis Vuitton tote added a designer touch.

Just what she needed: The usually serious Reese was in a lighthearted mood that she wanted to share with all following her jet ride home to LA on Monday

Glowing: The This Means War actress was looking relaxed and refreshed following her sightseeing holiday in the French capital

The actress was followed by her mini-me daughter Ava, 13, who was similarly clad in jeans, white Converse trainers and sunglasses.

Animal lover Ava also added a T-shirt emblazoned with the engaging face of a cat.

Reese was undoubtedly looking forward to seeing husband Jim and their eight-month-old baby boy Tennessee, and son Deacon, nine, who along with Ava is her child with former husband Ryan Phillippe.

Reese had a wonderful time in the French capital with just her daughter, and on Sunday Reese and Ava were spotted touring the City of Lights armed with a camera.

Happy to be home: Reese's goofy smile said it all as Ava trailed behind her

She and her teenager casually wandered around Paris dressed down in jeans, ankle boots and jackets.

Reese and Ava picked up a few souvenirs as they visited the buzzing metropolitan area.

By now a world traveller, Reese was only too happy to show her daughter the sights of Paris such as Places des Vosges, Rivoli Street, and the shops of the Marais quarter.

At the end of the afternoon they were seen emerging from the Centre Georges Pompidou, which boasts a large public library, the largest museum of modern art in Europe, and a centre for music and acoustic research.

Travelling in style: She's usually so reserved but on this day Reese was casually hip in jeans, grey sweater, scarf and cowgirl boots

What a difference a vacation makes: Reese was looking tired and weary on May 31 when she left LAX with Ava, bound for Paris
