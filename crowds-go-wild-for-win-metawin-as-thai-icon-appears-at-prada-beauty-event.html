Crowds go wild for Win Metawin as Thai icon appears at Prada Beauty event

Thai icon Win Metawin is making the crowd go crazy everywhere he appears!

Win Metawin debuted his Thai showbiz career with the BL drama 2gether and the rest is history. The 24-year-old superstar showed off his diverse range of acting appearing as the main lead in multiple hit dramas including F4 Thailand: Boys Over Flowers, Devil Sister, Enigma, and more.

Win Metawin knows how to capture millions of hearts just with one appearance and his latest attendance at Singapore’s Prada Beauty store opening is proving the same! Deemed as the top fashion icon of Thailand, the Beauty Newbie actor’s suave look for the event featured a stunning Prada suit and a contrasting pastel knit.

Right before Win’s appearance, cameras captured the chant of fans as all eyes waited for the Enigma actor to arrive! Win appeared wearing his usual warm smile and welcomed fans to the first-ever physical Prada Beauty store in Singapore.

Win trends all over the world

Win fans all over the world had a lot to swoon over after anticipating for days for the Prada event. The actor’s name trended on Twitter/ X in over 20 countries and gushing on Win’s impact on a crowd, a fan wrote: “These pictures talk more, when just a little boy with many dreams has grown into a man who does many things, fulfill his dreams one by one with his own hands, please don’t stop to make us proud you our PRADA GBA Win Metawin Opas-iamkajron!”

Another pointed out the obvious:

Win is the perfect match for Prada as a third fan posted: “Prada x Win never disappoints. The perfect combo to ever exist!”

Win’s award-winning acting journey

Along with his global popularity as the face of Thai showbiz, the actor is also recognized for his acting as he recently won the Thai Male Super Star award in 2023.

Earlier, Win also won the Line TV Awards 2021 Best Rising Star, Kazz Awards 2021 attractive Young Man Of The Year award, and many more.
