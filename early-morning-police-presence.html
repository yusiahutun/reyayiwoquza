Man arrested after early morning Davenport police presence

UPDATE, Sept. 1, 2 p.m.

A Bettendorf man was arrested early this morning on several misdemeanor charges after a heavy police presence at the intersection of Fourth and Harrison in Davenport.

Logan Wesley Garrison, age 18, was arrested on charges including Criminal Mischief 5th Degree, two counts of Eluding, Failure to Display a Registration Plate and No Insurance. He was released on bond. No further information is available at this time.

EARLIER: There was a heavy police presence in downtown Davenport early Thursday morning.

This was near 4th and Harrison around 4 a.m.

Our Local 4 News crew saw multiple police officers and vehicles.

One vehicle appeared to have been in a crash with a white car travelling on Harrison.

At this time we do not know if there were any injuries or arrests.

We will bring you more information on this incident as it becomes available.
