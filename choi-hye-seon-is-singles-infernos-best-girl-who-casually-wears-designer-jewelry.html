Choi Hye Seon is Singles Infernos best girl who casually wears designer jewelry

Choi Hye Seon is one of the first six singletons Netflix viewers get to meet in Singles Inferno season 3 episode 1. She’s bringing her bubbly personality and body positivity to the show as she attempts to find love. Season 3 not only has a new cast but some fresh twists and turns in its first four episodes fans haven’t seen before.

Three men and women arrive at Inferno Island in episode 1 of the Netflix show. Before they’re able to really get to know each other, some are whisked off to Paradise. Choi Hye Seon has to pick who she wants to go to Paradise with out of Choi Min-Woo, Lee Jin-Seok, and Lee Gwan-Hee. If her chosen partner picks her as well, then they both get to experience living in luxury on Single’s Inferno.

Meet Choi Hye Seon

Introducing herself on Singles Inferno season 3, Choi Hye Seon explains she has a “bubbly personality.”

She adds people refer to her as “the human vitamin.”

The “quirky” singleton adds it’s not only her personality which gives her confidence, but she’s also very “confident in her proportions.”

The 26-year-old works out five days a week and says she often gets asked for her number while at the gym.

She’s looking for a ‘manly man’

Single Inferno season 3 episode 1 sees Hye Seon explain her type is “manly men with great bodies.”

The Netflix star is no stranger to being asked out.

But, now she’s ready to find love the Singles Inferno way. She’s ready to “awaken all of the dating cells lying dormant.”

The Singles Inferno hosts noticed Hye Seon seemed “approachable” as she “smiled a lot” and they assumed she’d be one of the more popular women on the Netflix show.

Hye Seon already looks to be very popular with Netflix viewers as some have dubbed her the “best girl” on the show, as well as calling her “loveable.”

One fan commented on a Reddit thread: “Choi Hye Seon casually wearing Vivienne Westwood to breakfast…”

Another wrote on X (Twitter): “Omg she’s so cute, her character so interesting.”

Get to know Singles Inferno star on Instagram

Singles Inferno season 3 cast member Hye Seon can be found on Instagram with almost 13k followers at @hazelchoiii.

She explains in episode 1 she’s a college student who is majoring in bioinformatics in the Life Sciences department. Hye Seon is attending Ewha Women’s University.

She’s currently doing a remote internship for a Silicon Valley startup company.

As well as having a super-impressive career ahead of her, Hye Seon appears to do some modeling, judging by her Instagram page.
