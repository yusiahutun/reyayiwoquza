Rebel Wilson looks so different in new swimsuit photo with daughter

Rebel Wilson must be on cloud nine as a new mom and newly engaged woman. 

The Australian actress, 43, appears to be relishing in family life and shared a snapshot of a candid moment with her baby girl, Royce, this week.

In the image posted on Instagram, Rebel was enjoying a dip in the ocean but her reliable and fierce swimsuit poses had been replaced with soft motherly instincts as she glided her baby through the water.

The mom-of-one protected her skin from the sun with a full-body suit which had similar hues to her child's. Royce looked adorable in a pink swimsuit and white sunhat which had been secured underneath her chin. 

Rebel caption the post: "Roycie’s first ever swim in the ocean," and added a blue heart emoji.
