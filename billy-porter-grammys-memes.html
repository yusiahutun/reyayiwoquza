11 Funniest Memes of Billy Porter's Grammys Hat

  • Billy Porter arrived to the 2020 Grammy Awards in a blue crystal Baja East jumpsuit and matching fringed hat by Sarah Sokol Millinery.
  • The row of dangling crystals hanging from his hat brim was mechanically powered to part and reveal his face.
  • Naturally, the Internet memed the impressive headpiece.

The Grammys red carpet is not for the faint of heart, and that's exactly why sartorial king Billy Porter crushed it. The Pose star—known for astonishing outfits like his Oscars tuxedo dress and Met Gala sun god ensemble—brought his A game to tonight's ceremony in a custom, blue crystal jumpsuit by Baja East, complete with a matching cropped jacket, silver platform shoes, and tiered crystal fringe dangling from the hems of his pants.

The real focal point of the look, however, was the mechanically-retracting fringe bucket hat, made in collaboration with Sarah Sokol, which alluded to the the milliner's line of "paparazzi-proof" headpieces. But if inconspicuousness was what Porter was going for, he achieved the complete opposite with this look. (It has over 70,000 hand-placed crystals, for crying out loud.)

After Porter's hat fringe magically parted ways to reveal his face, the Internet became quickly fixated. The headpiece inspired a meme that sums up how you feel when you're nosy or just ready to stir the pot.

Porter, already a Grammy Winner from his time on Broadway's Kinky Boots, wasn't nominated for any awards this year (though he did introduce a performance). His hat, however, deserves a trophy of some kind.
