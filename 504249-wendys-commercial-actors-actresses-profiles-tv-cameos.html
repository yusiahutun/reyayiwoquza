1. Kathryn Feeney

  • Full name: Kathryn Feeney
  • Date of birth: 4th December 1976
  • Age: 46 years old as of (2023)
  • Profession: Actress

Kathryn Feeney is an American actress in Wendy's commercial. She is also a writer and has been featured in the films Where'd You Go, Bernadette and Mr Iglesias on Netflix. Kathryn is also known by her alias, that girl from Eat Fresh Wendy's commercials that she appeared in 2014.

2. Morgan Smith

  • Full name: Morgan Smith Goodwin
  • Date of birth: 29th March 1986
  • Age: 37 years old as of (2023)
  • Profession: Actress

Morgan Smith is widely known as Red, Wendy's Girl, from the fries and burger franchise Wendy's commercial aired from 2012 to 2016. She is also known for her film exploits on Messiah, Veep, and Boardwalk Empire.

She gained nationwide publicity following her selection to be the face of the Wendy's Restaurant ad campaign Now That's Better. Morgan, the dark blonde actress, became a household name with her recognisable Orange Red hair.

3. Austin Basis

  • Full name: Austin Lee Basis
  • Date of birth: 14th September 1976
  • Age: 46 years old as of (2023)
  • Profession: Actor

The Brooklyn, New York-born actor Austin once worked as a bartender, bouncer, and substitute teacher as a struggling actor back in the day. While Jewish, Austin majored in theatre at the University of Binghamton, undertaking minor roles in performances such as Rosencrantz &Guildenstern are Dead and I Hate Hamlet.

His breakout role on television came when he was cast for screenplay on the incarnation film Beauty & the Beast as J.T Forbes. Aside from acting, he has appeared in ad commercials such as Wendy's, Dr Pepper, Burger King and Feed the Pig.

Dandrell Scott is among the most recognisable Wendy's commercial actors with fast food retail as a voiceover actor at Wendy's since 2020. He began voicing commercials in 2018 after hiring by Walmart, Comcast and Ford to feature in their commercials. After a successful spell with the food franchises and building a reputation, Dandrell landed a role at Wendy's, featuring in their breakfast commercial.

5. Clara Peller

  • Full name: Clara Peller
  • Date of birth: 4th August 1902
  • Age: 85 years (Deceased)
  • Profession: Tv personality, manicurist

Clara Peller is a pioneer Wendy's fast food restaurant chain commercial actress. The late Clara Peller starred in 1984 Where's the Beef Wendy's commercial. Initially a manicurist Clara's unique voice, coupled with her no-nonsense persona, made her quickly garner attention, becoming a fan favourite among many.

Clara's feature in Wendy's commercial catchphrase was so popular across the masses in the U.S. that it made her a household name. After her mantra caught on, Clara made appearances in numerous other advertisements. Some commercials include the Campbell Soup Company Prego Pasta Plug Spaghetti sauce advertisement, but none became as popular as her Wendy's cameo.
