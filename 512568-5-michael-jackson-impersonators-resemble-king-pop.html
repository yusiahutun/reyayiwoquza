5 Michael Jackson impersonators who resemble the King of Pop

Over the years, several people have impersonated him and played roles in movies and TV series. So, who are these Michael Jackson impersonators who resemble him?

An impersonator is a person who pretends to be someone else for entertainment or even fraud. Many celebrities, including the late Michael Jackson, have been impersonated. Michael Jackson's impersonators appeared so real that many fans believed they were him.

1. Navi Charles

Navi is one of the people who look like Micheal Jackson. For more than 24 years, he has reigned as the number one MJ impersonator. He has performed many of his songs in 150 concerts in 70 countries.

Navi was so good that he became a close friend to the King of Pop. He even invited him to his estate and hired him as a decoy to distract the paparazzi. Not many people could distinguish the two.

Navi is a Trinidadian actor based in England. He was born on February 4th,1976, in Trinidad & Tobago. He moved with his family to London, where he developed an interest in pop music and was a fan of Micheal Jackson.

Navi Charles acted as Jackson in Michael Jackson: Searching for Neverland. The American television biopic was released in 2017. Not only does he dance like the late King of Pop, but he sings, too.

3. Michael Trapson

If you list people that look like Michael Jackson, the name Michael Trapson cannot miss from the list. He is an American musical artist and actor. He was born on January 20th, 1991, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
